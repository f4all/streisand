Background

"The Hellenic Authority for Communication Security and Privacy is an authority with administrative independency. ADAE is based in Athens, but can decide to establish and function offices in other cities of Greece. ADAE is responsible to submit its decisions to the Minister of Justice. At the end of every year, all the activities performed and the actions taken by ADAE are submitted to the President of the Parliament, the Minister of Justice and the Greek parliament."
