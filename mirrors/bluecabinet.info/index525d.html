Products

Products page

Nvigil

Nvigil is a data leakage prevention solution that resides at network level and monitors the traffic flowing through all the channels. It carries out deep inspection of packets with no impact on network performance. It reports the traffic, violating internet policy and blocks it instantly. Thus the sensitive company data is not leaked out.

Manage ISMS

ManageISMS is a web-based one-stop solution that helps organizations establish and maintain ISMS as per ISO/IEC 27001:2005 standard in an easy and intuitive manner.

Automate your ISO 27001 compliance activities such as Asset Management, Risk Management, Internal Audit, Management Review, Document Management, Effectiveness Metrics Management, Reports and Analysis with ManageISMS that makes ISMS compliance a snap!

Cognite

Cognite is a platform to impart organizational wide security knowledge to multiple user groups, from end users, to system administrators, software developers and top management. It has a variety of built-in multi-lingual security courses, which can further be customized to each organization's requirements. It provides feedback to measure and monitor progress of users in assimilating security knowledge.

Prexio

Prexio is an automated tool to conduct comprehensive configuration and policy audits on your servers and network devices. It is a packaged product that ensures compliance with internal standard hardening and configuration policies.
