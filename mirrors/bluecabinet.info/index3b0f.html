Background

"Saudi Ericsson is a private limited liability company (100% Saudi Company), founded in July 1, 1980 as a joint venture between E. A. Juffali & Brothers and L. M. Ericsson of Sweden. (It took over the activities of EPPCO, a pioneer in the supply of telecommunication systems in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia since 1964).

Saudi Ericsson is able to service its large customer base through a well established infrastructure of three main branches; Riyadh, Jeddah and Dammam, supported by 400 professional employees.

Saudi Ericsson is committed to provide solutions, integrates systems and services and to shape them into profitable investment for our clients.

Saudi Ericsson supplies a complete range of communication products and services in eight major categories: Legacy, Mobile and IP-PABX Systems, Contact Center Application, Radio and Microwave Systems, Transport and Transmission Equipments, Data Communication, Wireless Mobility, Banking Trading Systems, Healthcare Nurse Call System and all associated required applications.

Saudi Ericsson's communications systems are found in major government departments, leading hospitals, universities and private organizations throughout the Kingdom." For more information: e-mail at: www.marketing@saudi-ericsson.com Zawya

  • 1980: Saudi Ericsson venture partnership

"Saudi Ericsson is a partner to Ericsson, Aastra, CISCO and IPC among other brands' in KSA. The company has installed hundreds of LogFinity installations on top of these global PBXs vendors, from TDM to IP. LogFinity is also integrated with many other well known PBX vendors and telephony environment. “We are happy to extend our relationship with Saudi Ericsson; Saudi Ericsson is a pioneer telecommunication systems supplier in the kingdom of Saudi Arabia since 1964' .” Said Waseem Al-Ghanem Founder and CEO of TeleFinity.

  • 2009 Aastra Technologies distributorship agreement

"Aastra Telecom Middle East is the Middle East & North African business unit of Aastra Technologies Limited, (TSX:"AAH"), a leading company at the forefront of the enterprise communication market. Headquartered in Concord, Ontario, Canada, Aastra develops and delivers innovative communications products and applications for businesses. Aastra's operations are truly global with more than 50 million installed lines around the world and a direct and indirect presence in more than 100 countries. Aastra is entirely dedicated to enterprise communications and offers one of the most complete portfolios of unified communications solutions individually tailored to satisfy its customers' requirements. These range from feature-rich call managers for small and medium businesses and highly scalable ones for large enterprises, integrated mobility, call centre solutions to a wide selection of terminals. With a strong focus on open standards, Aastra enables enterprises to communicate and collaborate more efficiently."
