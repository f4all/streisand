Background

Private Company

"We are profitable, privately funded and employee owned, with corporate headquarters in San Diego and an EMEA sales office in France...

JumpGen Systems focuses on Network and Telecom Equipment Manufacturers; we have a track record supporting Alcatel Lucent, Ericsson, Motorola and numerous Tier 2 TEMs.

We also provide solutions to defense companies, and to other Embedded Board Manufacturers. Recent applications include line rate deep packet inspection and low latency platforms for algorithmic financial trading." JumpGen
