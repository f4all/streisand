Background

  • For all office locations and contact info


"Net Optics is the leading provider of Intelligent Access and Monitoring Architecture solutions that deliver real-time IT visibility, monitoring and control. As a result, businesses achieve peak performance in network analytics and security. More than 7,000 enterprises, service providers and government organizations—including 85 percent of the Fortune 100—trust Net Optics’ comprehensive smart access hardware and software solutions to plan, scale and future-proof their networks through an easy-to-use interface. Net Optics maintains a global presence through leading OEM partner and reseller networks."

"Net Optics, Inc., the leading provider of Intelligent Access and Monitoring Architecture solutions delivering real-time IT visibility, monitoring and control; and RDT Equipment and Systems, a supplier at the forefront of Automatic Testing Equipment, Communications, Test & Measurement and Wireless & RF, will host a seminar in Tel Aviv, Israel, to address business and technology aspects of Network Intelligence.

At the seminar, Sharon Besser, Net Optics Vice President of Technology, will discuss how Network Intelligence enables companies to gain visibility in virtual and physical environments for improving management, security and compliance.

Network Intelligence—Vital to Next-Generation Networks

Network Intelligence is an overarching technology that builds on the concepts and capabilities of network monitoring, Packet Capture, Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) and Business Intelligence (BI). Network Intelligence enables an organization to closely examine the volumes of IP data that cross communications networks. This is accomplished by identifying the protocol elements used and extracting packet content and metadata for analysis of data relationships and communications patterns.

Network Intelligence Delivers the Benefits of Monitoring Technology Progress

Network Intelligence is used to capture and feed information to a host of monitoring tools, support security and compliance, and carry out network management and instrumentation, as well as for bandwidth management, traffic shaping, policy management, and lawful interception activities. Currently, Network Intelligence is being incorporated into a wide range of applications by vendors who provide technology solutions to enterprises, Communications Service Providers (CSPs), governments and Small-to-Medium Businesses (SMBs)."

Board of Directors

  • Eldad Matityahu, Chairman of the Board

Eldad Matityahu is the Founder and Chairman of the Board for Net Optics. He created Net Optics in April 1996 to provide innovative, state-of-the-art technology for customers to Tap into their Network.

Fueled by Eldad Matityahu's commitment to customers, dedication to innovation, and drive for excellence, Net Optics has achieved global recognition for its cutting-edge passive monitoring solutions, and partnerships with the leading security and network management companies. Under his direction, Net Optics has been profitable for every quarter since its inception. The company continues to post double-digit growth after having doubled in size for each of its first five years.

Eldad Matityahu's vision and leadership shape the strategy at Net Optics. With his guidance, the company has expanded in ways that create bold, new products that anticipate Net Optics' customers' needs. A primary goal is to support the Net Optics team with the best possible tools and opportunities for success, so that they can better serve Net Optics customers.

Prior to founding Net Optics, Eldad Matityahu provided marketing and business development leadership for AMP, now a division of TYCO, Incorporated. His background includes several entrepreneurial endeavors including the development and sale of multiple global ventures. Eldad Matityahu studied Business Administration at San Jose State University.



  • Bob Shaw, President and CEO

As President and Chief Executive Officer of Net Optics since March of 2001, Bob Shaw implements the company vision and strategy. He motivates the executive team to stay focused on helping Net Optics customers win in their markets. Under his leadership, Net Optics has achieved consistent double-digit growth, launching more than 25 new products, acquiring over 700 new customers, and expanding Net Optics' global presence.

Shaw brings both Fortune 200 management and start-up experience to Net Optics. Prior to leading Net Optics, Bob Shaw was Sr. Vice President of Sales for Noosh, a market leader in collaborative enterprise communication for the Fortune 500.

In addition, Shaw has held Senior Vice President leadership positions within sales, marketing, and operations for the $1 billion business unit at RR Donnelley & Sons Company. During his years at Donnelley, he also served as a general manager at one of their state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, where he guided 300 employees to reach new performance benchmarks for the company.

Shaw holds both a Bachelor of Arts degree in Business and a Bachelor of Science degree in Economics from Geneva College in Pennsylvania.


  • Burt Podbere, CFO

Burt joins Net Optics with more than 20 years of experience in senior management, finance, accounting, global operations, public offerings, and mergers and acquisitions for small-cap and Fortune 500 companies. Most recently Burt served as CFO, Senior Vice President, Finance, and Chief Accounting Officer at Epocrates. During his four-and-a-half-year tenure, he completed nine financial statement audits and led the company through a successful IPO in early 2011. Before joining Epocrates, Burt served in a variety of senior finance roles at both Adterative and Symantec. He also spent over four years at Amdocs, a provider of software and services for medical billing, where he held several international roles including General Manager of Amdocs Software Systems Limited, based in Dublin, Ireland and Director of Finance for Amdocs Canada, Inc. At Amdocs, Burt designed and built two Shared Service Centers spanning all of EMEA and Canada. Burt articled at Ernst and Young in Toronto for over four years in their corporate audit practice. Burt is a Chartered Accountant and holds a B.A. in Economics from McGill University.
