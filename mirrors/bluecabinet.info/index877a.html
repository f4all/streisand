Background

Zhongxing Telecommunications Equipment Corporation (ZTEC) is the parent company of ZTE. [1]

  • ZTE UK is a subsidiary of ZTE Corporation (ZTEC)
  • ZTE USA is a subsidiary of ZTE Corporation (ZTEC)

ZTE is publicly traded, listed on both the Hong Kong and Shenzhen Stock Exchanges and is China’s largest listed telecoms equipment company.

Through its network of operators across 140 countries,connecting global customers via voice, data, multimedia and WLAN.

"ZTE has established 15 state-of-the-art R&D centers in the China, France, India and employs over 30,000 research professionals. Ten per cent of the company’s annual revenue is invested in R&D.

In addition, ZTE cooperates with its top-tier industry chain partners to build laboratories focused on developing technological breakthroughs.

ZTE ranked No.1 among global companies in terms of PCT patent filings in 2011 with 2,826 total applications. Also, the company ranked No. 1 in China in terms of the domestic patent authorization and application.

With 107 subsidiary organizations globally, ZTE wins the trust of global clients through its devotion to innovation, customization and high-quality services."
