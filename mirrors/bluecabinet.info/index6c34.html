Background

A Bull Group company.

"Amesys SAS provides hardware/software systems engineering and integration, product development, software development, technology consulting, and technical assistance services. It specializes in security and critical systems technology, signal processing, software development, cryptography, microelectronics, and embedded electronics. The company offers contactless readers, point of sale terminals, radio transmissions, and electromagnetic logs, as well as onboard equipment in the fields of speed measurement, odometer, data acquisition, and geolocalisation; digital and analogue electronics, physical measurement, electricity, automation, industrial data processing equipment, radioprotection, and surveillance machines; and embedded and ruggedized equipment, electronic warfare systems, transmission systems, weapons control systems, navigation instruments, network and security systems, security software, and physical network systems, as well as data acquisition, telemetry, and simulation equipment. It also manufactures satellite communication equipment, such as IP satellite routers for ISPs, network operators, system integrators, VARs, content providers, and Internet service providers and their customers. In addition, the company provides outsourcing, system and integration, network and security, offshore, and software development and test services. It serves defense and aeronautics, telecommunications, transportation, energy and industry, network and security, and microelectronics industries. The company was formerly known as i2e Technologies and changed its name to Amesys SAS in 2007.

The company was founded in 1979 and is based in Aix-en-Provence, France. It has locations in Aix en Provence, Paris, Toulouse, Grenoble, Rennes Sophia Antipolis, Lyon, Strasbourg, and Bordeaux, France, as well as in Morocco, Luxembourg, Canada, Germany, and Spain. As of January 18, 2010, Amesys SAS operates as a subsidiary of Bull SA." BusinessWeek
