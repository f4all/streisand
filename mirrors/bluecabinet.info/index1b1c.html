Background

"AREA is the Italian leading provider of technology solutions to support Law Enforcement Agencies in interception activities."


"In 1996 Andrea Formenti and Davide Perteghella, presently CEO and Director of R&D, founded AREA..."

The first and primary project of AREA is called MCR. It "is a comprehensive solution for global interception and analysis of voice, fax, video and data (Internet or private networks) on any type of wired and wireless connections.


"In 1997, with the first MCR release, AREA reached the goal of realizing a multi-channel digital audio recording system based on standard hardware and software components having the key value in the complete accessibility through the IP network."


"AREA has deployed 200+ MCR installations all over Italy and in other European Countries."


Area devotes approximately 80% of their focus on R&D, with a staff of 100 people.

Products

"MCR: THE GLOBAL INTERCEPTION AND ANALYSIS MANAGEMENT SYSTEM A suite of scalable components, based on LAN/WAN architectures and supported by professional services


  • MCR Server: the system core providing voice, fax, messages, video and data interception on any type of network (ISDN, PSTN, UMTS, GSM) including Internet (email, web pages, FTP transfer or voice) both from the ISP or the target line (analog, ADSL, wireless, ethernet). Most common protocols are supported and new ones are continuously added.


  • MCR Studio: a powerful graphic tool designed around MCR server in order to analyse recorded data coming from various sources allowing users to easily build their own analysis models. A set of international standard models are provided with the system.


  • MCR Office: specifically designed for supporting LEA in order to manage all admin tasks such as court authorizations, costs, suppliers and scheduled activities, providing a complete reporting system with full graphical capability.
