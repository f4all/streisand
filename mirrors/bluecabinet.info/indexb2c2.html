Products

All products include Deep Packet Inspection

  • Clearswift Secure Web Gateway
  • Clearswift Secure Email Gateway
  • MIMEsweeper for SMTPprovides Deep Content Inspection and Filtering

"Clearswift MIMEsweeper for SMTP is built on the same MIMEsweeper content-aware filtering technology that powers the Clearswift SECURE Web Gateway and SECURE Email Gateway.

It offers real-time bidirectional scanning of all email, meaning both incoming and outgoing messages are checked for viruses, malware and content confidentiality. Clearswift MIMEsweeper for SMTP performs deep analysis on Compressed and normal attachments, detecting and examining content even if files are embedded. By default, attempts to evade scanning using password-protected files or unauthorised encryption are blocked so no content evades Clearswift MIMEsweeper for SMTP's scrutiny... with Clearswift IMAGEmanager installed. Clearswift MIMEsweeper for SMTP monitors images exchanged via email to ensure legitimacy of content. Fast and reliable image-detection algorithms are able to detect and then quarantine pornographic and obscene images attachments. Clearswift IMAGEmanager can also identify and filter emailed images that containing confidential company information or intellectual property."

People Responsible

Board Members

  • Martin Leuw, Executive Chairman

Prior to joining the Clearswift Board, Martin served as Group CEO of IRIS from 2001-2011 and led its growth from revenues of £9m to over £120m.


  • Andy Balchin, Chief Financial Officer

Andy joined Clearswift in 2011. He has been in the software industry for 25 years, holding CFO and other senior finance positions in several enterprise level IT businesses.


  • Jeremy Hand, Non-Executive Director

Jeremy is co-founder of Lyceum Capital. Over the past 20 years, he has worked on numerous buy-outs, buy-ins and growth capital situations.


  • David Harland, Non-Executive Director

David leads Lyceum's operations team, working closely with portfolio company management. He has deep management experience in the UK and internationally in both the public and private sectors.


  • David England, Non-Executive Director

David has 20 years’ experience in senior roles at Computer Software Group, Solution 6 Group and Tyco International, with a particular focus on performance improvement, M&A and international growth. He is currently a non-executive director of Access.


Management Team

  • Martin Leuw, Executive Chairman

Prior to joining the Clearswift Board, Martin served as Group CEO of IRIS from 2001-2011 and led its growth from revenues of £9m to over £120m.


  • Andy Balchin, Chief Finance Officer

Andy joined Clearswift in 2011. He has been in the software industry for 25 years having previously held CFO and other senior finance positions in several IT sector enterprise level businesses.
