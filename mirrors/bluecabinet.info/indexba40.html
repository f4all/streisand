Products

InteliCENTER

Abstract: InteliCENTER is software/hardware? which provides automated and manual real time data mining and data analysis from structured and unstructured digital sources.

Data Input: emails, phone calls, statistics and reports etc.

Data Output: raw data, reports, trends, trends, relationships, alerts, statistics and what if scenarios.

Data Output Continued: Scenario Modeling: production of possible scenarios from available data.

Monitor Patterns, Trends and Deviations: generation of trends and highlighting of anomalies.

Timely Reports, Alerts and Alarms: the software running on the database can continually produce reports, and alerts when it determines it necessary to do so.

Dossier Management: definition, maintenance and viewing of details of entities, suspects, locations, victims, object etc. used in crime, modus operandi etc.

Cluster Analysis: definition and management of entire cluster of people from millions of entities in minutes and shows groups defined on the basis of desired parameters and describes linkages within a group and highlights the strongest and weakest links.

Social Network Analysis: maps networks and sub-networks of entities and people using Link Analysis and analyses common and hidden suspects associated with multiple events, people etc.

Coordinator Number: identification of coordinators and pattern discovery for people acting as coordinators between a head and a group of its associates on the basis of their communication behavior.

Entity Geographical Profiling: use of Location Pattern Analysis and creation of timeline analysis of people’s communications and financial transactions from various locations during a time period and tracks people's movements on the map for defined time periods.

Shared Entities: listing of all objects used for communication and transactions by people such as shared handsets, shared SIM cards, account numbers, etc.

Parametric Query Analysis: ability for the researcher to define their own query with desired combination of parameters and does analysis based on own investigation approach. Customization of the query for doing a time series or spatio-temporal analysis for the same case or across cases.

International Transactions/Communications: identification of people and entities involved in transnational organized and coordinated activities using international call details analysis and fund transfer record analysis of desired entities and explores entities visiting/communicating/transacting with desired set of countries; performs trend analysis over their behavior.

Silent Entity Analysis: highlighting of abnormal behavior such as silent or dead activity during defined time periods.

Platform: unknown, it runs on Oracle Database 11g R2, Oracle Exadata and Oracle linux 5.5.

Target Market: It is intended for Law Enforcement Agencies and Intelligence Agencies and Policy Makers.

Note: This product includes knowledge curation, similar to iCurator.

Proactive Intelligence and Analysis "To address the above mentioned challenges, Xalted offers Proactive Intelligence and Analysis solution powered by InteliCENTER especially designed for Law Enforcement / Intelligence Agencies. iCURATOR

Abstract: InteliCURATOR is software/hardware? which provides automated knowledge curation, which is a form data analysis that presents data and metadata from structured and unstructured digital sources as consistent and with different levels of abstraction. InteliCURATOR provides efficient long term data retention and fast data access to down stream applications.

Data Input: emails, phone calls, statistics and reports etc.

Data Output: curated data in a consistent format that can be viewed at different levels of abstraction.

Data Output Continued:

Dossier Management: allowance of definition, maintenance and viewing of details of entities, people, locations, objects, modus operandi, etc under a case.

Person/Location Data Management: Records and updates a target's metadata such as personal details, communication details, cases and groups associations, etc. and maintains and monitors location data, highlights hotspots and sensitive locations enabling fast data retrieval.

Platform: unknown

Target Market: It is intended for Law Enforcement Agencies and Intelligence Agencies and Policy Makers.
