Background

The company’s products are used for command, control, communications, computer, and intelligence applications.

Tadiran Spectralink Ltd. [1], the Communication and Data Links Division of Elisra, is a highly specialized producer of advanced wireless communication systems." [2]

Tadiran Spectralink Ltd. and Tadiran Electronic Systems Ltd. operates as a subsidiaries of Elisra Group." Source: Bloomberg (See Elisra Group)

"Tadiran's systems are in service with the Israel Defense Forces and other modern armies worldwide. They assist countries across the world in managing Spectrum Control..."


  • " Tadiran Communications, Ltd. operates as a subsidiary of Elbit Systems Ltd."

"Tadiran Communications, Ltd. designs and develops defense communication systems and equipments. The company engages in radio frequency (RF) design and development in frequencies ranging from 1.5 MHz - 5 GHz, Spread Spectrum techniques, crypto algorithms, wireless data transfer application modems, error detection and correction adapted to radio channels, advanced synchronization techniques, communications protocols, and radio channel control." [3] [4]


  • 1962: Koor creates the Tadrian electronics joint venture.
  • 1968: The Israeli Defense Ministry sold its 50 percent interest in Tadiran to America's General Telephone and Electronics Corporation (GTE).
  • 1999: Tadiran Communications, a division of Tadiran, was sold to the Shamrock group, First Israel Mezzanine Investors, and a group of managers. Five years later, in 2004, Koor bought back a 33 percent stake with the intent of merging its business with Elisra, reported Defense Daily International. The $140 million to $150 million Koor paid for its one-third stake was equal to the value of the whole company when it had been sold off five years earlier.

[5]

"Tadiran Electronic Systems Ltd. designs, develops, and manufactures military electronics systems for the defense forces and army. Its products include signal intelligence and communication jamming, spectrum control, divisional artillery command and control, battalion level c4I artillery, auxiliary, tactical ballistic missile defense, voice communication, man-pack, multi-sensor compatible c4ISR, and tactical remote receiver systems, as well as ground-based reception stations for airborne platforms, ship borne receiving stations, and communication management and switching subsystems."

People Responsible

Tadiran Spectralink Ltd. Key Executives

  • Itzhak Beni, CEO and President

Itzhak Beni serves as President of Elisra Group.

Mr. Beni also serves as the President and Chief Executive Officer of Tadiran Electronic Systems and Tadiran Spectralink Ltd.

Previously, Mr. Beni served a as General Manager of the Data Link Division of Tadiran´s Communications Group.

In this capacity, he led the transition of the division into the independent Tadiran Spectralink Ltd.

Mr. Beni is a Lt. Col. in the Israeli Air Force (IAF) reserves, and was Commanding Officer of a special Air Force unit before retiring from active service.

He holds a degree in Electronics Engineering from the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, Haifa.


  • Yehudit Rozenberg, CFO and Senior Director of Finance & Control

Yehudit Rozenberg serves as the Senior Director of Finance & Control and Chief Financial Officer of Tadiran Spectralink Ltd. Mrs. Rozenberg serves as the Director of Financial of Tadiran Electronic Systems Ltd.

She held number of managerial positions with diverse organizations within Tadiran Ltd.: Director of Finance of the Communications and Systems Groups; Comptroller of the Communications Systems Division; Head of the Finance and Customers Department of Tadiran´s Corporate Headquarters, etc.

She serves as a Member of The Board of Taldor Computer Systems (1986) Ltd.

Mrs. Rozenberg has Executive MBA from Tel Aviv University and MA degree in Economics from Bar-Ilan University.


  • Itzhak Mor, Senior Director of Operations & Customer Support

Mr. Itzhak Mor has been President of Telkoor Telecom Ltd. since his joining in January 2010.

Mr. Mor served as Senior Director of Operations & Customer Support of Tadiran Spectralink Ltd. since 1984 and also served as its Vice President of Operations.

Mr. Mor served as Senior Director of Operations & Customer Service of Tadiran Electronic Systems Ltd.

Mr. Mor served as a Lt. Col. (Res.) in the Israel Defense Forces (IDF).

He held numerous managerial positions in some of Israel´s largest defense and commercial corporations. Mr. Mor holds a degree in electronic engineering from Tel Aviv University, And B.A. degree in Business Administration from Derby University.


  • Eli Bortman, Senior Director of Research & Development

Eli Bortman serves as Senior Director of Research & Development of Tadiran Spectralink, Ltd.

Prior to joining Tadiran Spectralink, Mr. Bortman filled senior executive positions with some of Israel's leading hi-tech companies - most recently at Alvarion, where he was Vice President for R&D and Systems.

Before that, Eli was Vice President, R&D at Bamboo Mediacasting and served as Vice President and General Manager of Celtro - the Cellular Business Unit of ECI NGTS.

Previously, from 1987 to 2000, Mr. Bortman had filled responsible managerial and R&D positions at ECI Telecom.

He holds a BSc degree in Electronics Engineering and an MBA degree from Tel Aviv University - both Magna Cum Laude.


  • Zvi Krepel, General Manager

Zvi Krepel serves as General Manager of Communications Division and Vice President of Elisra Electronic Systems Ltd.

Mr. Krepel serves as Vice President, General Manager of Communications Division and Member of the Management Board of Elisra Group.

He serves as General Manager of Tadiran Spectralink Ltd. He served as Director of Spectralink´s Strategic Business Units (SBUs) for Search & Rescue and Guided Weapon Systems, and for Data Links for UAVs and Space.

He served as Director of the Communications & Navigation Department at Israeli Air Force (IAF) and its Director of the Defense Research & Development Division.

He was a Col. in the Israeli Air Force (IAF). After 27 years of active service, he filled numerous important positions.

Mr. Krepel holds degrees in Electronics Engineering (BsC) and Business Administration (MBA).
