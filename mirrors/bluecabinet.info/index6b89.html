Products

Mobile surveillance

Prism 200a handheld through-wall radar, which has been designed to be used by police, special forces or the emergency services.

It provides quick and covert intelligence on the movement and location of people in a room or building - without the need for invasive sensors. Prism 200 has been designed for situations where a high degree of insight is essential for success.

Should be noted that Prism 200 can be used for good purposes, location of earthquake survivors for example, or in a hostage situation. The technology is neutral and Cambridge Consultants are D&D not manufacturers, however they did market this previously at DSEIto look for investors. DSEI is a defence and security equipment exhibition held every two years in London Docklands, which draws thousands of visitors, both trade and military.
