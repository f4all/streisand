Background

"SS8 Networks is an established global provider of highly sophisticated lawful interception and communication forensics solutions in the multi-billion dollar cyber security market. SS8’s solutions allow law enforcement agencies to conduct lawful interception and communication monitoring of both circuit and packet-mode communications, extract related metadata, accurately reconstruct all targeted voice and data communications, such as telephone calls, emails, chat sessions, web surfing, and provide comprehensive analysis of communications patterns – all in accordance with local laws and standards...

SS8 solutions serve the communication interception and forensics needs of local, regional and national law enforcement and intelligence agencies to effectively and efficiently monitor electronic correspondence in today’s cyber world and apprehend terrorists and criminals in the real world.

SS8 leverages its extensive knowledge of network operations, and ever-changing Internet protocols to extract communications content and related metadata from the network, reconstruct it and provide the analytical tools required to create a true “end-to-end” solution. In the last 10 years, we have developed a series of innovative industry “firsts” thereby positioning SS8 as an industry leader in the lawful interception and communication forensics markets. Our expertise in the telecommunications area has enabled us to bridge the path between the service providers and the LEA and intelligence community worldwide."


"SS8 is a leader in communications intercept and a worldwide provider of regulatory compliant, electronic intercept and surveillance solutions. Headquartered in the heart of the Silicon Valley in Milpitas, California, SS8 has a global reach and is an independent provider of regulatory-compliant electronic surveillance solutions that have been deployed in over 25 countries for the largest wire-line, wireless, cable, VoIP and satellite service providers. These voice and data installations can intercept more than 100 million subscribers, and serve local, regional and national law enforcement and intelligence agencies worldwide. SS8 products are sold and supported both directly and through OEM partnerships." ISS World
