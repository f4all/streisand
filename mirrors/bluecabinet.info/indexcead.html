Background

Company Registration Number 634 3869

Gamma Group includes, Gamma TSE, G2 Systems, Gamma International and Gamma Group International.

Gamma International is currently best known for their IT intrusion software FinFisher


  • "FinFisher is security software that has stirred controversy because Gamma International marketed it to government security officials who were told it could be covertly installed on suspects' computers through exploiting security lapses in the update procedures of non-suspect software. Egyptian dissidents who ransacked the office's of Egypt's secret police following the overthrow of Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak reported they discovered a contract with Gamma International for £287,000 for a license to run the FinFisher software." Wikipedia

The FinFisher product portfolio is solely offered to Law Enforcement and Intelligence Agencies.

The FinFisher suite can be used as individual products and when interconnected give intelligence agencies advanced tools for unsurpassed IT investigation and surveillance techniques within the IT environment."

  • Gamma Group International, (Middle East and Asia Offices) established in 1990, provides advanced technical surveillance and monitoring solutions and international consultancy to National and State Intelligence Departments and Law Enforcement Agencies. Gamma is a European-based company with its headquarters in the United Kingdom and subsidiary offices in Germany, the Middle East and South East Asia. Our aim is to provide comprehensive communications monitoring solutions and technical consultancy services, as well as professional training for our customers worldwide. The common aim of all Law Enforcement Agencies is to have state-of-the-art capabilities to intercept all kinds of communications within different telecommunication networks and carriers inside and outside a country’s borders. • GSM, GPRS, and UMTS Tactical Monitoring and Locating
• Passive Monitoring of Telephone Lines

• Speech Identifying Tools, Data Retention and Link Analysis
• Radio Frequency Monitoring
