Background

  • MorphoTrak Inc. is a private company, along with other "Morpho" companies, owned by the Safran Group, France.
  • MorphoTrak Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Morpho SA.
  • The company was founded in 1985 and is based in Alexandria, Virginia with corporate facilities in Albany, New York; Anaheim, California; and Tacoma, Washington. It has regional facilities in the United States.
  • The company provides its solutions for people, companies, and governments worldwide. MorphoTrak Inc. was formerly known as Sagem Morpho, Inc. and changed its name to MorphoTrak Inc. in April 2010. [1]

"Safran continues to apply its strategy of becoming a global leader in the security market, by acquiring the biometrics business of Motorola’s Printrak division, which becomes MorphoTrak, then 81% of General Electric’s Homeland Protection business. Following the purchase of the American company L-1 Identity Solutions, in 2011 Safran creates MorphoTrust USA, Inc." [2]

"MorphoTrak (Safran group) is the world leader in multibiometric technologies for fingerprint, iris and facial recognition, and an acknowledged expert in identification systems. Its solutions meet a wide range of security needs for people, companies and governments worldwide, including identity management, personal rights for residents and travelers, physical access to airports or other high-value sites, and logical access, either online or via secure terminals." [3]


MorphoTrak Inc. designs, manufactures, and integrates biometric and identity management solutions.

  • It offers airport and transportation security systems;
  • biometric system integration services;
  • law enforcement AFIS-type systems and services;
  • mobile IDs for border management, law enforcement, and commercial applications; and multi-modal biometric systems for fingerprint, face, iris, palm, and latent prints.

The company also provides collection devices, services, and concepts;

  • personal identity products and services, including identity cards and smart cards;
  • physical and logical access control products and services; and security automation processes.
  • In addition, it offers forensic systems;
  • federal government security, port security, airport security, and border security solutions;
  • and identity management and commercial security solutions, and access software applications.

[4]

Products

  • MetaMatcher

"The core of Morpho’s biometric solutions is the MetaMatcher, the fastest and most accurate matching software on the market. It enables all types of biometric comparisons with other biometric databases (fingerprint, palm print, etc…).

Using a highly distributed architecture, MetaMatcher is a software-based solution (no hardware dependency) that demonstrates the very high speed and accurate matching capability that is now achievable on standard computer hardware. The MetaMatcher can handle databases of several hundred million of biometric records. A single laptop running MetaMatcher software can match more than three (3) million personal records per second using ten fingerprint records and more than two (2) million faces per second.

This is a tremendous improvement over the classic centralized architecture that requires a higher network bandwidth. It also eliminates the risk of bottlenecks when processing very high throughputs. Consequently, MetaMatcher’s functionality can be extended beyond the constraints of centralized architectures.

How is this speed possible? We have achieved this extraordinary matching speed by using intelligent database exploration and optimized multi-step matching algorithms, while placing the templates in live memory. In addition, multi-core architecture, hyper-threading and other features, such as pipe-lining, have all contributed to increased speed. We’re committed to creating innovative solutions and drawing maximum benefit from all technological improvements. A highly effective multi-biometric matching engine for challenging identification situations MetaMatcher"

- Proven multi-biometric matching architecture (fingerprint, palm print, unsolved latents, facial, iris, trans-jurisdictional databases, etc...)

- High throughput and multi-million record database

- Scalable matching power for faster response time

- Fusion of matching algorithms for unrivaled accuracy

- Database in computer memory increases matcher speed

- 100% software matching solution

- COTS hardware for cost efficient technology update

- Seamless system expansion

- Optimal ownership cost [5]
