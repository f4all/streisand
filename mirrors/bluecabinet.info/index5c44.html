Background

Kapow Software, Inc. was formerly known as Kapow Technologies Inc.

Headquarters in California with additional offices in New York, Austin, Frankfurt, and Copenhagen.

"Kapow Software, Inc. operates as a self-service automation and integration company. The company also provides... Cloud integration, Web intelligence, mobile enablement, and analytics and business intelligence solutions.

In addition, Kapow Software, Inc. offers process automation, application integration, big data collection, mobile enablement, content migration, Web data intelligence, social media monitoring, and other solutions.

The company serves retail, entertainment, financial services, publishing, manufacturing, government, technology, health care, and HR industries in the Asia Pacific, the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, and Africa."
