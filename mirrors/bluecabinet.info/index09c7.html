Background

NetVeda is a privately-owned networking company. The engineering team is highly experienced in real time embedded systems and networking technologies. The mission of the company is to develop innovative solutions that provide granular traffic control for effective network management, improve quality of service (QoS) and optimize bandwidth resource utilization for Internet service providers, Small Office/Home Office (SOHO) and enterprise network environments.

"NetVeda has pioneered solutions with adaptive incremental technologies to provide cost-effective and reliable policy-based network access, QoS, content and transaction controls. Today's local area networks (LANs) are an active component of internet working. NetVeda's client-centric network traffic management solution is more efficient, flexible and scalable compared to traditional server-centric models. Enforcement of opaque user and application based access privileges at local endpoints, as opposed to imposition of requirements on network applications to supplement futuristic technologies, offers a viable solution to achieve better utilization of network resources. QoS is an end-to-end performance metric between the sender and receiver and must be programmable throughout the flow, not merely at intermediate nodes (routers and hosts) along the data paths. The centralized management and distributed enforcement of policies through stateful inspection of protocols and packet content is a powerful strategy to regulate networks and control traffic load for performance, efficiency and security."

In 2008 a legal claim was filed against NetVeda and other companies for Patent Infringement

Products

The flagship product family, Policy.Net, is ready-to-market and has successfully completed months of rigorous testing in real-world environments on all Windows platforms. Architecture diagram

"Policy.Net Enterprise offers a comprehensive traffic management solution, with distributed endpoint policy enforcement and centralized policy definition. Policy.Net provides endpoint network security, LAN and WAN bandwidth management, peer-to-peer rate controls, congestion management, intelligent inspection of content for filtering and blocking, site blocking, remote traffic recording, live traffic monitoring and reports."

NetVeda's unique multi-platform kernel mode solutions execute below the network layer to minimize operational overheads and render real time performance with minimal impact on system performance. Policy.Net technology raises the bar for endpoint security, with pioneering context-sensitive content filtering for protection of intellectual property, DNS tracking for reliable site based filtering, live traffic monitor for congestion and detailed reports for intrusion detection and network forensics.
