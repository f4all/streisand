Background

ANTMEDIA is a privately owned company that specializes in software, initially to small and medium businesses and, more recently to global corporations and governments worldwide, including NASA and USAID.

ANTAMEDIA provides "software solutions employed in more than 170 countries and translated in 30 languages. The company was founded in 2001 and since then it has had years of experience in cybercafe and hotspot business. Antamedia offers Internet Cafe software, HotSpot software, and the other products specially designed for cybercafes, hotspots and medical institutions throughout the world..." CNET

ANTMEDIA software, for Intenet Cafes, appears to be popular and used in Syria, Iran, Greece, Ireland and HYATT, Hilton, Holiday Inn and Sheraton hotels worldwide.

Software Development and Customization - "We are constantly engaged in meeting customers needs and requests. Development includes feature extensions, new features, integration with third-party products, user interface design, database integration, hardware interfacing, web applications."
