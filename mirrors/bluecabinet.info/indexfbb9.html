Background

CYBEROAM is a division of Elitecore Technologies, with the primary investor being The Carlyle Group.

"Elitecore Technologies is led by a strong management team with in-depth experience in the security domain. With 400+ employees, Elitecore has offices in USA, Middle East & India and has its R&D and Global Support Management Centre in India..."

"Elitecore Technologies Pvt. Ltd. provides OSS/BSS and Internet security solutions to configure, manage, and monitor network and subscriber activities.

It offers Cyberoam Endpoint Data Protection, a solution enabling organizations to execute centralized control over access, modification, and transfer of organizational data through management-defined roles for individual users/groups; and identity-based unified threat management appliances for network security applications in enterprise, SME, and SOHO markets.

The company also provides Cyberoam iView, an open source logging and reporting solution that offers visibility into activities within the organization for security, data confidentiality, and regulatory compliance; and

CRESTEL, an OSS BSS platform enabling data, voice, and video services over wire-line and wireless networks to manage application, media, and communication services offered directly and through distribution channels.

In addition, it offers Elite Authentication, Authorization, and Accounting (AAA), which manages the authentication of subscribers, authorizes them for appropriate level of service, and ensures accounting of their usage.

Further, the company provides Netvertex, a policy manager that makes policy enforcement decisions in real time based on services, applications, network resources, subscriber profile, and SLA to ensure QoS and bandwidth allocation; and

24online Access Gateway, a billing and bandwidth management solution that enables broadband, dialup, WiMAX and Wi-Fi service providers, hotels, hotspots, and cafes to provide time and usage-based Internet access and manage subscribers and franchisees.

It offers its products to telecommunication, network and data security, and access gateway markets through resellers worldwide.

Elitecore Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is based in Ahmedabad, India with additional offices in India, the United States, Bahrain, Dubai, and South Africa." Businessweek
