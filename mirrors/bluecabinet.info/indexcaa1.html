Background

SYBORG is a subsidiary company of Verint, with their parent company Comverse Technology Inc (CTI). As of 07/24/2000, Syborg Informationsysteme GmbH operates as a subsidiary of Comverse Technology, Inc.

SYBORG claims to be "The German expert for Lawful Interception"

Specialised applications in the telecommunications sector Our key expertise: Complete systems for the interception of telephony services and all types of data traffic.

SYBORG Informationssysteme has been certified according to DIN EN ISO 9001:2008

"Syborg Informationsysteme GmbH provides systems for the interception of telephony services and data traffic.

It offers SYBORG interception centre (SIC), an integrated solution, which is used to intercept voice, data, and other Internet traffic in single system and analyze the data coming from landline, mobile, or broadband network;

SGate, a solution for telecommunications carriers that provide functions for data collection and routing;

SARS, a tracing system, which is used to trace subscribers automatically; and

interception test recorder that is used to test network components for conformity.

The company also provides tactical solutions, including GI2-GSM identity interrogator, a portable system, which is used to capture and process international mobile subscriber identity and international mobile equipment identity;

SIC for Internet service providers, a system used for interception in IP networks;

SIC for WiFi; and tapping equipment, including ADSL lines, ATM recorders, high-speed modem connections, passive IP tapping, ISDN BRI lines, and SYBORG ISDN recorders.

In addition, it offers services, including conception and consulting; presentation of products and live demonstrations for planned systems; software development for individual systems and adaptation to technical conditions; project management, installation, and start-up; operational support; maintenance of existing systems; and systems leasing.

The company was founded in 1991 and is based in Bexbach, Germany. " Businessweek

  • ATM Recorder

" The ATM Recorder serves to intercept Internet activity directly at the Internet Service Provider, or to capture data directly within the networks. The data for the target facility (usually DSL) are recorded in the ATM network of the provider and routed straight to the input interface of the ATM recorder. All data are saved in a default directory and are then accessible for further processing. The data can be decoded on the spot and analysed immediately when combined with the SYBORG Data Analyzer, or can be transmitted to the SYBORG Interception Centre for further processing. "


  • Passive IP Tapping (IP Probe)

The IP Probe system for intercepting broadband traffic can intercept up to 9 lines with data rates up to 1 GBit / sec (also optical) with one single IP Probe, without any loss of packets. The filter algorithms only process the relevant traffic. Thanks to integrated decoding and routing it is possible to carry out recording and further processing in any monitoring centre that conforms to ETSI (e.g. SIC from SYBORG). Flexible interception criteria enable the probe to be used in almost any broadband network for the interception of:

IP addresses

URLs and host names

VoIP

Email (addresses or user name in SMTP, POP3, IMAP4)

Webmail

Chat and Instant Messaging (IRC, MSN, Yahoo, ICQ, FTP, Telnet, News)

All the filter criteria can be set on the spot and become active immediately.


  • SIR

Intercepting telecommunications in businesses or hotels entails both legal and technical problems as only one target needs to be intercepted. Since the typical solution is overall interception of one PRI line, technical implementation involves considerable effort and is often not feasible on legal grounds.

With the SIR (SYBORG ISDN Recorder) it is now possible to record on one ISDN PRI line (30 B channels) directly and to filter according to specific numbers. This method ensures that only communications for the target facility are recorded (e.g. in a hotel room). This drastically decreases the technical outlay needed to carry out the interception measure and ensures that the recording is target-centric, which simplifies enforcement of court orders for interception measures like this.

The SIR consists of a portable PC, which is connected passively to an ISDN line and records all telephone calls for the specified phone numbers. The SIR also has a facility for listening to calls directly and can duplicate the calls to another telephone line - so the investigator can if necessary move around freely with his mobile phone and can also listen in to any telecommunications even when in the vicinity of the target. This makes it possible for surveillance teams to take action within a minimum of time.

The SIR can also be equipped with the additional SDA software (SYBORG Data Analyzer), which makes it possible to decode and view fax, modem and Internet sessions in the SIR directly. The recorded communications can of course also be transmitted to the Syborg Interception Centre for further processing."
