Background

Products

Dual Usage Strategy of Lawful Interception Systems

  • Septier Information Explorer : Septier solutions are built to collect massive amounts of raw data and information. The data is stored in dedicated fast performing storage devices utilizing hundreds of terabytes per system. Collected information may include CDRs, IPDRs, Location updates, SMS etc. By utilizing pre-programmed reports and analytical tools it is possible to perform nontrivial extraction of implicit, previously unknown, and potentially useful information from the collected data. Our Data mining solutions involve sorting through large amounts of data and picking out relevant information.
  • Septier Tactical : Septier Tactical Package is a comprehensive set of products that ensures you the total control over cellular communications in the covered area.
  • Septier Location Tracking
    • Septier GMLC : Accurate location tracking of a mobile subscriber is one of the security-related challenges in today`s world. With numerous networks in various frequencies stretching across continents, a fool proof setup is required that can perform accurately and effectively. Septier Gateway Mobile Location Center is an essential component in Septier Active Location System, which offers efficient, accurate and easy to operate location tracking capabilities to mobile network owners and law enforcement agencies.
    • Septier Accurate Location
      • Assisted Global Positioning System: Septier AGPS is a technology that utilizes the combination of the GPS and network data in order to accurately locate a targeted mobile device.
      • Network Measure Report Processing: Septier NMR locates the targeted subscriber via signals from the nearby operating devices. The accumulated signals are then analyzed by the software that cross references the signal results with topographical, geodesic and other data and returns the location of the target mobile device with high accuracy.
      • Radio Frequency Fingerprinting: RF Fingerprinting is another method that is based on NMR data. However, instead of "direct" analysis of the accumulated signals, it works on what-if basis. Assuming certain location of the targeted subscriber, an alleged signal map is generated. These signals are then compared to the signals of the "real" device – the one to be located. Iteratively making corrections to those assumptions, the exact location of the target device is identified.
  • Septier Call Detail Record Generation : The primary advantage of using Septier CDR is its ability to collect data from any infrastructure or AMA source through customized efforts. Further, the storage capabilities of the technology are almost thirty times higher than the conventional methodologies, enabling storage of data up to two years backwards. Septier CDR does not require any upgrade of its environment upon necessary scaling of the data. It also adds value to telecom companies by supporting all functionalities of SQL through a wizard based interface that is easy to understand and requires minimal setup.
