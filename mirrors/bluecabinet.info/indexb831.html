Background

Sasken Communication Technologies Limited, an embedded communications solutions company, provides telecommunication software services and solutions to network equipment manufacturers, mobile terminal vendors, and semiconductor companies worldwide. It offers IC design services for the wireless, communications, consumer, automotive, networking, and defense sectors; hardware design, phone design, and handset testing services; smartdevice frameworks that include services for Android, Symbian, Windows Phone, Maemo, and Meego, as well as solutions for smartphones, tablets, set top-boxes, and SmartTVs; and technology solutions for network equipment providers and services across the lifecycle of network equipment. The company also offers network engineering services, such as network planning, deployment, commissioning, integration, and network operations support to network equipment vendors and operators; multimedia solutions comprising software, frameworks, and applications that enable the delivery of multimedia content for chipsets and devices; GSM/GPRS/EDGE/WCDMA pre-integrated 3GPP wireless protocol stacks, as well as development, platform integration, field readiness, maintenance, and support services for wireless and cable modem protocol; and connectivity services, such as component development/enhancement, platform integration, testing and validation, application development, and connectivity combo chip driver/middleware development and integration.

Products

  • NFC Firmware

Near Field Communication (NFC) Firmware is designed for Semiconductor customers who want to create NFC enabled chipsets and need a standard compliant, highly versatile and efficient off-the-shelf software.[1]

  • Wireless Protocol Stacks

Sasken Wireless Protocol Stacks offer customers complete layer 2, layer 3 and layer 4 implementation pre-integrated on our partner’s chipsets. The solution conforms to the latest 3GPP Release 99.[2]

  • Application Framework

Sasken Application Framework is a flexible, platform-agnostic, scalable software solution that enables the rapid development of differentiated feature phones across platforms and phone segments.[3]

  • Multimedia Subsystems

Sasken’s multimedia application suite is a complete range of multimedia applications and codecs that enable handset manufacturers to bring out sharply differentiated products in a cluttered marketplace.[4]
