"Huawei Investment & Holding Co., Ltd. provides information and communication technologies solutions for enterprises in China and internationally.

The company operates in three segments: Carrier Network, Enterprise Business, and Consumer Business.

The Carrier Network segment develops and manufactures a range of wireless networks, fixed networks, telecom software, and core networks, as well as services solutions to telecommunications operators.

The Enterprise Business segment develops integratable information and communications technology products and solutions, including enterprise network infrastructure, cloud-based green data centers, enterprise information security, and unified communication and collaboration solutions to various industries comprising governments, public utilities, enterprises, energy, power, transportation, and finance.

The Consumer Business segment develops and manufactures mobile broadband devices, home devices, smartphones, and related applications for consumers and businesses.

Huawei Investment & Holding Co., Ltd. has a strategic memorandum of understanding with Intel Corporation.

The company was formerly known as Shenzhen Huawei Investment & Holding Co., Ltd.

Huawei Investment & Holding Co., Ltd. was founded in 1987 and is based in Shenzhen, China." Businessweek

Video: Interview with Ihab Ghattas, Assistant President, Middle East Region, Huawei


Mr. Ihab Ghattas has been Assistant President of Middle East Operations for Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. since September 2008.

Mr. Ghattas is responsible for driving Huawei's business in the region besides being instrumental in developing strategies related to marketing, human resources, social activities and administration.

He is also responsible for establishing and developing key relationships with clients and operators across the region.

Mr. Ghattas is a senior telecommunications professional and has over 28 years experience in the telecoms industry. He served as Chief Business Development Officer (CBDO) of Huawei Technologies, and was responsible for establishing and developing key relationships with operators across the MENA region, as well as promoting and introducing Huawei's Enterprise Solutions to businesses outside of the telecommunications arena.

Prior to joining Huawei, Mr. Ghattas spent most of his career with Etisalat, a leading operator in the MENA region. During his career with Etisalat, he has covered many areas of the responsibilities in the areas of projects implementation operation & maintenance, and finally network planning for both wireline and wireless networks.

Mr. Ghattas is a graduate of Ain Shams University of Egypt with a BSc in Electrical and Electronic Engineering.
