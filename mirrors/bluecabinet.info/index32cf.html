Background

NetSweeper, Inc. provides Internet content filtering and Web threat management solutions and services for businesses, educational institutions, government organizations, telecommunication companies, Internet service providers, and OEM partners.

It offers Internet content filtering solution, a Web content categorization engine and Web-based administration tool that blocks and filters inappropriate content, viruses, malware, and bandwidth consuming applications;

  • Web security solution that identifies zero-hour and known Web-borne threats, such as phishing scams and other forms of malicious code at the Web gateway;
  • Application management solution for identifying and inspecting Internet applications at the packet level;
  • Mobile device protection solution that enables the enforcement of acceptable user policies regardless of where these devices access the Internet;
  • Student mobile management solution for students using school owned laptops on and off the primary network; and
  • NS PROx Web filter appliance, which enables the implementation of the company’s Internet content filtering solutions.

NetSweeper, Inc. also provides technical support services related to software installation, configuration, and usage. The company’s solutions protect Internet users from inappropriate and destructive Web content, as well as enable IT managers to protect networks from malicious attacks and their organizations from legal liability risks.

It serves customers through a network of distributors and resellers in the United States, the Middle East, South America, and across the APAC region.

The company was incorporated in 1998 and is based in Guelph, Canada with regional offices in the United Kingdom, India, and Australia.

Products - NetSweeper and DeepNines

Internet Monitoring, Filtering, Blocking, Deep Packet Inspection, Integration

Some of the solution features as described on the NetSweeper website:

  • Web 2.0 Protection :

Netsweeper’s packet-level filtering addresses individual URIs, not just websites.
This means that the mashups and embedded content in web pages can be categorized individually, rather than relying on the category of the aggregating page.

  • Keyword Blocking :

In addition to our dynamic categorization, Netsweeper also provides intelligent keyword blocking throughout the solution.

  • SSL and HTTPS filtering :

In addition to HTTP filtering, Netsweeper’s unique approach to encrypted traffic filtering enables fast and effective filtering without jeopardizing security certificates.

  • Block Anonymous Proxies :

Netsweeper identifies and blocks more than 40,000 new proxy sites every day. :
Netsweeper allows enforcement of SafeSearch capabilities across Google, Yahoo, Bing, YouTube, easysearch.org.uk, Metacrawler, excite.com, search.lycos.com, AOL, Dogpile, Hotbot, Alltheweb, ask.com and playaudiovideo.com.

  • Protocol Management :

Operating at the protocol level, Netsweeper Mobile Filtering allows administrators to control more than just HTTP traffic; you can also control the use of file-sharing programs, messaging programs, voice over-ip (VOIP) protocols, and more.

  • Integration and Interception with ImageStream router
    • See also DeepNines Technology by NetSweeper
  • DeepNines Remote Filtering "Provides complete visibility into user activity regardless of location"
  • DeepNines Network Application Monitor provides real-time, advanced network visibility with historical reporting across all ports and protocols. As an intelligent application monitor, it identifies all activities and applications for the Web to provide complete network visibility and awareness for analyzing..."
  • DeepNines Proxy Blocker technology was also originally developed by DeepNines, and is the only technology able to recognize and block any type of Web proxy or other circumvention attempts at the gateway... DeepNines Proxy Blocker detects and eliminates Web-based proxies that circumvent network security and filtering solutions. By identifying, reporting and preventing Web proxies in real-time, Proxy Blocker protects against users bypassing content filtering, security policies and malware detection across all ports and protocols. And since the DeepNines approach is based on detecting the fundamental technology used by the proxy, Proxy Blocker can immediately block all Web proxies without knowing the specific IP address or URL.
  • DeepNines Content Inspector "By utilizing DeepNines patented deep packet inspection technology, Content Inspector provides real-time Web scanning of all content with near-zero latency..."

As network users browse the Web, the network traffic is intercepted by an ImageStream router, just like a transparent proxy. The router identifies the URL and sends a message to a Netsweeper Policy Server that is deployed within the local network and connected to the Internet. The Policy Server classifies the URL and replies to the router with directions to block the URL or allow transfer of the page to the client. "

Under Perry's leadership, Netsweeper has developed a team of internationally qualified professionals that continue to drive the business forward with a customer first attitude.

Today, Perry is the driving force behind his management team as he focuses on building a tactical road map for the business while developing strategic partnerships in the pursuit of new business opportunities. Perry is also actively involved in the industry of Internet safety and sits on the Board of Directors for associations such as ITAC (Information Technology Association of Canada) and KINSA (Kids Internet Safety Alliance) whose mission is to "help find, rescue and heal child victims of abuse whose images are shared on the Internet."
