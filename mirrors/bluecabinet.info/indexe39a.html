Background

Raytheon Trusted Computer Solutions, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Raytheon Intelligence and Information Systems. Raytheon Trusted Computer Solutions, Inc. was formerly known as Trusted Computer Solutions, Inc. and changed its name to Raytheon Trusted Computer Solutions, Inc. in November 2010.

Raytheon Trusted Computer Solutions, Inc. develops and delivers cross domain and cyber security solutions that enable government and civilian agencies to share and access information securely across multiple classified networks.

The company’s customers represent a range of agencies and operations within the intelligence community, department of defense, homeland security, civilian agencies, and commercial sector in the United States and internationally." BusinessWeek

Products

  • High Speed Guard, a software solution that enables highly complex, bi-directional, and automated data transfers between multiple domains;
  • SimShield, a secret and below interoperability certified fixed-format guard that provides the capability to label, segregate, protect, and exchange data between systems;
  • Trusted Gateway System, which provides built-in manual review and automatic validations, such as virus scanning, file type verification, dirty word search, and deep content inspection enabling safe and simultaneous data movement between networks.
  • Trusted Thin Client that provides end users access to various allowable networks from a single pane of glass pane;
  • WebShield, a commercial-off-the-shelf data guard that provides secure Web search and browse-down capabilities;
  • Security Blanket, a tool that automatically configures the client operating systems. In addition, it provides strategic planning and implementation services, as well as application management, support, and enhancement services;
  • Consulting services, including designing and delivering custom services-led solutions; and support services.
