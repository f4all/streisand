Background

    • Serco Group plc.is parent company of Serco Inc. and Serco Services, Inc.
    • Following a management buyout in 1987, RCA Services Limited was renamed Serco Limited. In 1988, the company achieved a full listing on the London Stock Exchange as Serco Group plc.


"Serco began life in 1929 when Radio Corporation of America formed a UK subsidiary, RCA Services Limited, to support the country's growing cinema industry. In the early 1960s the company won a piece of work that was to shape its future direction - a maintenance contract for the UK Ballistic Missile Early Warning System at the Royal Air Force (RAF) base at Fylingdales. This expanded into a contract to operate the technical facilities at the base and provide comprehensive support to the RAF - which we continue to do today. In the 1980s the UK Ministry of Defence began a programme of outsourcing and contracting for the operation and maintenance of defence installations and facilities - and it used Fylingdales as the model.

"Serco Inc. is a leading provider of professional, technology, and management services focused on the federal government. Our customer-first approach, robust portfolio of services, and global experience enable us to respond with innovative solutions that achieve outcomes that matter. Serco Inc. is part of an international service company with employees around the world. "

As of December 29, 2008, Serco Services, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Serco, Inc.

The company was formerly known as SI International, Inc. and changed its name to Serco Services, Inc. in December 2008.

"Serco Services, Inc. provides information technology and network solutions to the federal government in the United States. It offers solutions in various practice areas, such as program management and acquisition support for developing and managing government-required documentation, solicitation packages, source selections, and supplying contract management support; and integrated solutions development to integrate commercial-off-the-shelf products with custom software engineering.

The company’s practice areas also include systems engineering to construct solutions that address the prerequisites of net-centric environment, where service-oriented architectures dominate the network; business process and information technology outsourcing; and network solutions, which design, engineer, deploy, and manage networked communications and infrastructure solutions.

In addition, its practice areas include information security that delivers analyses, methods, and technologies to enable clients to secure their information against unauthorized access and service disruption; and logistics, which provide logistics support, acquisition support, program management, and related information technology services.

Further, the company’s practice areas include records management work in the areas of application processing, data entry, case and file management, identification and credentialing systems, call center support, and analytical support services; and learning and performance solutions to manage the change that occurs in the joint force context.

Its clients include department of defense, federal civilian agencies, and federal judiciary....

The company was founded in 1998 and is based in Reston, Virginia. As of December 29, 2008, Serco Services, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Serco, Inc." BusinessWeek
