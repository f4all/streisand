Background

"Wedge Networks provides high performance network based web security solutions to enterprises and service providers worldwide. Our technology leadership in Deep Content Inspection allows organizations to protect against new and emerging web based threats that traditional scanning methods have difficulty intercepting and controlling. Specialized in malicious-code detection and filtering at the application layer, our solutions touch millions of users and provide protection for utilities, service providers, health care, oil and gas, government, web sites, and many other enterprise customers." Wedge

Products

  • Wedge OS Deep Content Inspection.

"Deep Content Inspection is considered the evolution of Deep Packet Inspection with the ability to look at what the actual content contains..." Wikipedia

The WedgeOS NDP architecture revolutionized Web Security Appliances with the introduction of BeSecure.

  • "BeSecure is capable of intercepting and actively scanning all internet traffic for malicious content as it enters the network. Available in six models, organizations large and small can now provide the best protection from Internet threats without impacting network performance." Wedge
  • BeSecure Web Filter "enables organizations to understand, filter, monitor, and report on internet usage..." Integrates with Sawmill 8.5 and Juniper Networks, "Junos Content Encore." White Paper Report pdf

Company Directors

  • Daniel Freedman, Chairman

Mr. Freedman has an M.Sc. in Computer Science from University of Calgary, and since 1981 has founded, grown, managed, sold, integrated, and funded software companies in California, Texas, and Canada. Mr. Freedman’s unique combination of hands-on experience in small company entrepreneurship, large US public company executive management, and technical software development has most recently been applied at real-time communications startup, Jasomi Networks, which he founded. Jasomi creates technologies that solve deployment and regulatory problems for IP networks, accelerating their adoption, and powering the IP-based telecommunications revolution. Prior to founding Jasomi, Mr. Freedman was co-founder of Launchworks, Canada’s fastest-growing venture investment house during the late 1990s, and Vice-President of Security Products at McAfee Corporation, a role he earned after the acquisition of his network security and encryption company, FSA Corporation, by McAfee in 1996.


  • Hongwen Zhang, Director

Dr. Zhang co-founded Wedge Networks in 2005. Prior to being appointed President and CEO, Dr. Zhang was Chief Technology Officer at Wedge and was instrumental in developing the high performance architecture that provides the basis behind Wedge’s award-winning security appliance, the BeSecure Web Gateway. He holds a Ph.D in Computer Science from the University of Calgary; a M.Sc. in Computer Engineering from the Institute of Computer Technology - Chinese Academy of Sciences (Beijing, PRC), and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Fudan University (Shanghai, PRC). With more than two decades of high tech leadership experience, Dr. Zhang is a co-inventor and holder of several patents in the area of computing and networking. Prior to establishing Wedge Network’s, he was a co-founder of the 24C Group Inc., which pioneered the first digital receipts infrastructure for secure electronic commerce. Dr. Zhang was previously principal of Servidium Inc., now ThoughtWorks Canada Inc., a global leader in agile development methodology.


  • Husam Kinawi, Director

Dr. Kinawi is a co-founder of Wedge Networks and has a Ph.D and Master of Science in Computer Science from the Universities of Calgary, Canada and London, UK. He also co-founded Mpower Technologies Inc., a wireless telecommunications software company, in 1997 and went on to co-found ActiveIq.com (NASDAQ: AIQT), a Boston-based e-Business applications firm, in 1999. Dr. Kinawi is an innovator with more than 17 years of research and development experience working with industry leaders such as Newbridge (Alcatel), Siemens, United Technologies, and Apple in the areas of distributed information systems, embedded applications and wireless Internet solutions. He has been a guest speaker at numerous major conferences, published several research papers and is the holder of multiple patents in the area of mobile and wireless devices.


  • Eugene Chen, Director

Mr. Chen is a partner in Gowlings Lafleur Henderson LLP practicing in the Corporate Finance, Securities and Public M&A National Practice Group'. Mr. Chen has more than 15 years experience in advising companies on initial public offerings, private placement financings, corporate governance matters, securities reporting, general regulatory compliance issues, private equity funds, purchase and sale transactions, limited partnership offerings, private mutual fund trusts offerings, qualifying transactions, shareholder agreements and corporate restructuring and reorganizations. Prior to joining Gowlings, Mr. Chen was a partner at a large national business and litigation law firm. He has provided advice to a wide variety of business sectors such as: oil & gas, alternative energy, manufacturing, biotechnology and life sciences, pharmaceutical, health care, telecommunications, information technology, mineral exploration, software development and real estate. He is also a current and past director and officer of a number of private and public companies.


  • Mike Jomaa, Director

Mr. Jomaa is a successful entrepreneur and CEO of multiple companies. One of his key accomplishments is the founding of Modern Beauty Supplies 25 years ago. Modern Beauty Supplies is recognized as one of Canada’s largest beauty products distributors, with hundreds of employees. Mr. Jomaa’s ventures have been recognized with many awards including regional finalist for Canada’s 50 Best Managed Companies and top 3 Finalist for the RBC Small Business of Year. Furthermore, Mr. Jomaa is the owner and operator of Modern Solutions, which provides support and software development to numerous companies with business solutions and software needs. In addition to these prosperous business endeavors, he is an expert in sales and distribution, has 8 years of Real Estate experience and has found success in the real-estate development industry.

  • Joe Haar, VP Sales - Wedge Networks USA

Mr. Haar is a Silicon Valley Sales Professional with a 15+ year record of success pioneering technology start-ups. He has generated over half a billion dollars in revenue for early stage companies.   • Johnson Wu, GM - Wedge Networks USA

Mr. Wu, who now heads up Wedge’s expansion in the USA, was one of the original engineers of Cisco’s PIX Firewall and was a co-founder of Network Translation, Inc. (acquired by Cisco in 1995). He holds two patents for inventions in the area of network address translation for VoIP and streaming media that are used in PIX. Wu more recently designed and wrote the prototype of the first CPE-less SIP NAT/Firewall traversal device, previously thought to be impossible. The Prototype was later productized by Jasomi Networks as Peerpoint Centrex, the first commercially available Session Border Controller for far end NAT traversal. Prior to Jasomi, Mr. Wu held design and implementation responsibilities for Caw Networks’ award winning WebAvalanche and WebReflector capacity assessment products. Mr. Wu graduated Summa Cum Laude from the University of San Francisco, with degrees in Business Administration and Management Information Systems.


  • Owen Peng, GM - Wedge Networks China

Mr. Peng has over 20 years of experience in software and IT product development, project management and company operations. Prior to taking on the role of General Manager of Wedge’s China operations, Mr. Peng was the Vice President of VanceInfo, one of the largest IT service providers in China, listed on the NYSE. He has worked in a variety of companies in the U.S., Singapore, China and Hong Kong (Xerox and Lenovo, to name a couple) in the roles of CTO, VP of Engineering, VP of Marketing, Chief Engineer, Project Director, and senior engineer, along with being a Vice President and Co-Founder of SureKAM, a leading system integrator and IT services provider in China. Mr. Peng has rich experiences in the IT Industry, with expertise in Telecom BSS, Business Intelligence, Enterprise Software, Printing Systems, Software Testing and Software Engineering, and now spearheads Wedge’s expansion and growth in China. Mr. Peng received his Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence from the Academy of Automation Research of MMI in 1991, and his B.S. degree in Computer & Automation in 1984 from Xiangtan University.


  • Hussein Amery, VP Business Development

Dr. Amery is Wedge Networks’ Vice President of Business Development. Dr. Amery has over twenty five years of experience building business in different industries – oil and gas, real estate development, finance and IT. Dr. Amery’s most recent position was the VP Operations for a publically-traded managed Internet services provider (Fatport, a division of Skyboard Media). Prior to that Dr. Amery was a founding director of Ittihad Capital Corp., a boutique finance and leasing corporation. Dr. Amery completed his undergraduate education in Canada and his post-graduate education in California, earning a doctorate in psychology. While not working, Dr. Amery volunteers his time extensively to local and international charitable and non-profit organizations.


  • Cameron Schaus, Director of Engineering

Mr. Schaus manages the technical support team and R&D activities for the Wedge BeSecure Web Gateway and Reporter products, including content and feature management, and oversees development and release planning, product testing and outsourced QA activities. He has more than 10 years of experience working in the Information Technology industry in IT security services, network infrastructure design, systems development, telecommunication network design, embedded software development and quality assurance. Prior to joining Wedge, Mr. Schaus held key positions with Nortel Networks working on the development of cellular communication infrastructure, which was deployed and supported worldwide.
