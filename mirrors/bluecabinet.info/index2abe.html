Background

"RCS is one of the Leading Companies in the Lawful Interception market: a leadership that is confirmed by the relevant and growing operational presence in many countries around the world. Since the foundation, RCS has been developing a strong technological leadership in Lawful Interception software and data investigation solutions. Such a strong specialisation allows the company to heavily invest in Research and Development, pursuing excellence and capitalizing knowledge on any leading and growing technology relevant to the Lawful Interception and data analysis market. RCS has the headquarters in Italy and affiliates in France and Spain. RCS has more than 100 specialists mainly focused in the development and maintenance of Lawful Interception platforms along with Customers assistance."


Products

IP Traffic Analysis, Interception, Data Mining

  • [1] Investigations & Analysis Support Systems (SFERA)

Investigation oriented applications for investigative data mining and storage

"Capable of storing tens of hundreds of terabytes of investigation-related data of any kind, our Investigation Support Systems open the path to new forms of investigative analysis that were simply unthinkable just a couple of years ago. SFERA is a tool for investigation support based on the examination of phone traffic, geographical information and records. The system runs as a stand alone application able to analyse relationships, perform geographical and time mapping, statistics and special data searches..."


  • Advanced Internet Monitoring Centres

Automated recording, decoding, management and display of internet-based communications

" At the edge of the current technology, our Internet Monitoring Centres offer a complete suite for easy interpretation and investigation of Internet intercepted data. Constantly updated following the natural and quick evolution of Internet protocols and services, our IP Monitoring Centres offer a plain view of the activities carried out in the Net by the targets, covering all the major communication services available nowadays."


  • MONITORING CENTRES

"Whatever the size, our monitoring centres exploit the latest software technologies to offer state-of-the-art recording, storage, distribution and management of any type of telephone or audio-related intercepted contents."


Voice and Multimedia Lawful Interception platform includes:

  • MITO - Monitoring centre devoted to the storage, decoding, playback and full management of Lawful Interception recordings of any voice and multimedia source.
  • DFDM - Voice and data capture and distributor system used to intercept calls on PCM (2 Mbit/s) trunks and ISDN E1 ant T1 trunks.
  • MD - Mediation system that complies to any transport technology (including ISDN, SS7, CDMA, GSM, UMTS, GPRS, CDMA2000, SIP, H.323, MGCP).
  • AM/PS - Administration module and provisioning system devoted to generate LIID for new interceptions and carry out the provisioning by sending instructions to the monitoring system, configure targets, monitor target status on LEMFs, TIPs and NWOs’ Mediation Devices.


RCS Voice and Multimedia platform main features and characteristics:

  • Full handling of Mobile Phones interceptions (including multimedia and data contents such as MMS, Video calls and IP traffic), wire line interceptions and environmental and micro spies based interceptions.
  • Scalable architecture that allows to implement massive Monitoring Centres with thousands of parallel targets and serving at the same time the lower volume needs of smaller and highly specialized Law Enforcement Corps.
  • Integrable with the IP platform (RCS full-featured Internet decoding system) and SFERA (RCS business intelligence platform).
  • Compliance with all the major LI handover standards (ETSI, J-STD-025, GHIS, CALEA and a number of national and custom variants).
  • Organisation of targets by investigation and group policies.
  • Easy to use GUI running on standard Microsoft XP clients.
  • Secure encrypted communication between server and client. Secure access through the use of user/password or smartcards.
  • Easy and fast localization of mobile targets using smart Cell Data mapping engine.
