Background

"Optenet is an information security technology multinational based in Madrid (Spain). It specializes in developing solutions that enable individuals, enterprises, telecoms operators and ISPs to guarantee the security, availability and integrity of their information. The company was founded in 1997 and since then has gradually expanded its business lines and international presence."

List of Offices worldwide

"Optenet is a Spanish multi-national IT security company that leads the market in intelligent content recognition systems both in Europe and Latin America.

The company’s objective is to develop the best technological solutions on the market for the efficient and secure use of the Internet on any platform related to web environments and navigation on mobile telephones (WAP, SMS and MMS).

The company specializes in the integration of Security Systems into ISP (Internet Service Provider) network infrastructures and mobile telephony operators.

One of the company’s strengths is its revolutionary Online Semantic Content Analyser technology, a multi-lingual filter tool that is capable of analysing any information in real time based on artificial intelligence techniques. By using the same technology, the company develops innovative firewall, antispam and anti-phishing solutions, which, following an agreement with Kaspersky Lab, now include the most powerful antivirus and anti-spyware solution on the market." People Responsible

  • Francisco Martín Abreu,

Founder, Chief Executive Officer and President

He has worked as a researcher on numerous projects for international R&D companies, including the European Space Agency (ESA), Construcciones Aeronáuticas, S.A. (C.A.S.A), and the Center for Industrial Technological Development.

In addition, Abreu has participated in various government research projects, authored multiple publications and has a deep working knowledge of the IT field.

In 1997 he developed WebFilter, the first European Internet content filtering tool.

Mr. Abreu is an industrial engineer with a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering, Francisco.
