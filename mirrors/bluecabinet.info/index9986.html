Background

*As of November 26, 2012, Rsignia, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of The KEYW Holding Corporation.

"Rsignia is a leading provider of cyber security solutions and services including detection, mitigation, countermeasures, and forensics. In support of National Cyber Security Initiatives, Rsignia is developing some of the most innovative next generation cyber and forward thinking capabilities available to the marketplace today. Embracing the new cyber warfare paradigm, their long-standing relationships with United States government agencies, and growing commercial base has positioned Rsignia at the forefront of the rapidly growing cyber market." Rsignia


"Rsignia, Inc. provides cyber security solutions and services to governments, federal agencies, and commercial marketplace in the United States. Its solutions and services include detection, mitigation, countermeasures, and forensics.

The company offers CyWarfius security servers to acquire, record, analyze, filter, modify, send, and store data while delivering flow statistics and payloads to independent monitoring applications.

Its CyWarfius solutions include Packetjet, a traffic generation appliance comprising a packet generator engine and a configuration interface for packet generation and injection for testing network intrusion detection systems, firewalls, and IP stacks; and

Joint Forces Sensor to provide data manipulation capabilities for network monitoring and forensics analysis.

The company’s CyWarfius solutions also comprise CyberScope, a critical network warfare support system to search for, intercept, identify, and locate vulnerabilities while offering the capability to disrupt, deny, devastate, and delude the attacker. In addition, its CyWarfius solutions include rugged and deployable solutions, such as high end videographic solutions for the display, recording, and transmission of computer and video signals; and multi-image display processors, rugged flat panel displays, and high speed data recorders

Further, the company provides prime contractor support services-outsourcing, infrastructure solutions, and custom engineering."

  • 4.5.2012 "Rsignia, Inc. announced the availability of an affordable deep packet inspection network monitoring and forensics appliance, the Joint Forces Sensor(R) Lite (JFS). The fully configured small form factor appliance starts at $11,900, making it one of the most affordable in its class. The JFS Lite inspects all network traffic for real-time or post analysis. The system is also offered with various optional configurations as needed." Businessweek

  • 4.23.2012: Rsignia, Inc. and ReVera Gov, Inc. Form Strategic Alliance

"Rsignia, Inc. and ReVera Gov, Inc. announced a strategic alliance between the two companies. This alliance forges a mutually advantageous bond leveraging each other's respective technologies. Their capabilities converge to offer a cohesive and proactive network security offering that solves global problems surrounding new modes of communications. This strategic alliance enables the companies to develop and market their 'Alliance Solution Suite' of software and hardware products to customers worldwide. Each company brings expertise and capabilities of their specific components forming a unified solution that is a powerhouse of value to alliance customers. Furthermore, the two companies have agreed to contribute their expertise to jointly develop additional software and/or hardware capabilities connected with the solution suite. ReVera Gov will be integrating their software solutions modules that will monitor and analyze the new generation of network communication challenges with Rsignia's deep packet inspection proactive forensics platform. This converged appliance provides a unique approach to monitoring and gathering forensics data for the purpose of managing network bandwidth, capacity planning and network monitoring forensics."


People Responsible

Board Members

Darrell Covell, Founder, Chairman and Chief Technology Officer

"Mr. Darrell Covell founded Rsignia Inc.and serves as Chief Technology Officer and Chairman of the Board. Mr. Covell's background includes having started several Engineering based companies ranging from project-driven engineering services and integration specializing in rapid development, delivery, and qualification to deployments of complex data and signal processing systems and sub-systems. His expertise spans the range of sensor data applications, from acoustics to SDQ, techniques for sonar, radar, sigint, elint, communications, image feature extraction, and specifically in string search and packet capture forensics, developed by Rsignia today. He hold a B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering CIT, and has various certifications."

Mike Wannemacher, Chief Operating Office

"Mr. Wannemacher has held various leadership positions within the technical industry since the 1980's as well as being a successful entrepreneur. Over the past four years, he has implemented several successful key business strategies, has developed new business opportunities, and has provided productive insight and guiding principles in all aspects of management, sales and the overall corporate direction. Mike has a history of bringing new revenue streams and new partnerships to the company to allow for profitability, growth, and continued development of Rsignia's own IP."


John Fraize, Chief Scientist

"Mr. John Fraize serves as Chief Scientist of Rsignia, Inc. Mr. Fraize founded Isaac Research, Inc. He has 30 plus years R&D experience, including 12 years as electronics hardware engineer and 20 years as software engineer. He is a respected R&D engineer for key computer networking teams, which include Hewlett Packard, Digital Equipment, AT&T, and Apollo Networks."
