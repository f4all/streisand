Background

Paessler AG is a privately held company

Paessler AG develops, markets, distributes, and supports network monitoring and performance optimization software and solutions for small office/home office users, small and medium enterprises, government agencies, and universities in Canada, Great Britain, Japan, and the United States.

Paessler AG was founded in 1997 and is based in Nuremberg,Bavaria/Germany, with representatives in the U.S., Paessler develops, markets, distributes and supports software around the world. Backed by strategic partners across the globe, the company has sold more than 60,000 software licenses to date and has more than 150,000 active installations, including freeware products.

It offers PRTG Network Monitor V7, PRTG Traffic Grapher V6, and Webserver Stress Tool that enable data center providers, MSPs, and Internet service providers to prevent failures and outages before they take place, as well as to optimize network resources and reduce downtime. The company offers its products through resellers. Businessweek

Products

  • PRTG Network Monitor

PRTG Network Monitor runs on a Windows machine within your network, collecting various statistics from the machines, software, and devices which you designate. (It can also autodiscover them, helping you map out your network.) It also retains the data... so you can see historical performance, helping you react to changes. PRTG can collect data for almost anything of interest on your network.

  • Gerald Moser, VP of Business Development - UK

since July 2010.

Mr. Moser is tasked with shaping and expanding Paessler's presence in the UK - one of Europe's most influential information technology markets. Mr. Moser joined Paessler with more than 12 years of experience in business development and marketing communications for international technology and hi-tech businesses such as Fraunhofer and Dolby. He has a track record in introducing and driving take-up of innovative technologies in new markets and played a vital role in the launch of ground-breaking technologies, including the pioneering MP3 audio format.

Prior to Paessler, Mr. Moser held a number of business development and communications roles with market leading companies including Dolby, Coding Technologies and the Fraunhofer Institute for Integrated Circuits IIS, which pioneered the MP3.

For the success of his work, his team won the Future Prize for Technology and Innovation from the President of Germany. Mr. Moser has also co-authored a number of White Papers on introducing new technologies into the diverse markets.
