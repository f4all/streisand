Background

"6WIND is a privately owned company based near Paris, France with a US subsidiary in California, a sales and support office in Asia, and an R&D center in Beijing, China."

"6WIND was created as a spin-out from Thales , a world leader in cutting edge electronics serving the aerospace, defense and security markets. The company has a solid culture of industry-proven software innovation in communication systems design for defense and electronics networking."

" 6WIND provides high-performance packet processing software solutions used by leading suppliers of equipment, for cloud computing, enterprise networking and telecom infrastructure."

"6WIND has extended its lead as the #1 supplier of high-performance networking software for LTE infrastructure worldwide and 6WINDGate Mobile Edition software now serves 80% of LTE users in Japan. Beyond the technology strengths of the 6WINDGate software, OEMs adopt 6WIND’s solution because it is supported by a comprehensive ecosystem of compatible, industry-leading products from a wide range of partners. "

Tech & Business Partners

"6WIND partners with industry-leading suppliers of board-level products, operating systems and embedded software products worldwide."

6WINDGate has already been selected and deployed by companies such as Alcatel-Lucent, Ericsson, Hitachi, HP, LG-Ericsson, NEC, and Nokia-Siemens Networks, as a critical element of their products for mobile infrastructure, data center networks and high-frequency trading platforms.

"6WIND partners with processor vendors including Cavium Networks, Intel®, Broadcom®, Texas Instruments and Tilera® to port and integrate their networking protocols to the partner's multicore processor. These partnerships ensure that 6WINDGate is fully optimized for the Multicore Executives (MCEEs) that the processor vendors provide while also leveraging the capabilities of the on-chip accelerators included in their processor platforms such as security offload.

6WIND also partners with other vendors who offer complementary products including Mentor Graphics, MontaVista, Wind River, Metaswitch Networks, Red Hat, Qosmos and VMware.

Equipment vendors that provide boards and systems that utilize 6WIND software include Advantech,Emerson Network Power,GE Intelligent Platforms, Interphase, JumpGen Systems, Lanner, Narinet and RadiSys.
