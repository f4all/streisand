Tech & Business Partners

  • "Barbedwire has been a Partner with MSPs for two years, including membership in the Global MSP Network and now the MSP Alliance," said Zia Iqbal, CEO of Barbedwire. "We created the first ever MSP pricing model for our Unified Security Appliances in January of 2003, we added a Leasing option in June of 2004, so adding a monitoring service to our DP Inspector based solutions is a next logical step as we continue to innovate in the MSP market. The DP Monitor security service offerings will allow our MSPs, ISPs, and traditional VAR Partners to add a recurring revenue model in the very hot security management arena," said Dr. Humair Raza, Director of New Product Initiatives for Barbedwire." The Free Library


  • "Armando Garcia, CEO of Dicar Networks, San Jose, Calif., stated, "We recently signed as a strategic Partner with Barbedwire and are now successfully selling the complete line of their DP Inspector security appliances. I just placed a sizable stocking order for the DPI50W because we are forecasting a high demand for the unit based on early discussions with our customers. With a price point of $995 and a standard feature set that includes a secure wireless access point, firewall, VPN, AV, Antispam, URL filter, Deep Packet filter, a small office server and the ability to add advanced features now or later like an IDS, Vulnerability Scanner, SEMS, or WiFi/LAN Authentication, it is hard to beat."
