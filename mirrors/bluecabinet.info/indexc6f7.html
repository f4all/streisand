Background

"Datatec Limited provides information and communications technology (ICT) products, solutions, and services worldwide.

The company distributes advanced networking, security, and unified communications products to value added resellers, systems integrators, and service providers that resell networking products and solutions to small and medium-sized businesses, enterprise organizations, and governments.

It provides convergence, mobility, data centre network, videoconferencing, network storage, unified messaging, and network security products.

It provides design and configuration solutions of unified communications, mobility, and data centre networks, as well as network extensions, such as videoconferencing, network storage, unified messaging, and network security;

and various services for these solutions.

The company also offers ICT solutions and services with a focus on information technology infrastructure and networking solutions, communications and collaboration, data centre, cloud, and professional and managed services.

In addition, it offers integrated technology solutions for enterprise and medium-sized companies in industries, such as financial services, retail, manufacturing, and professional services, as well as to government.

Further, the company provides strategic, trusted advisory, modeling, and market intelligence services to the telecoms, information technology, and digital media industries;

networking services and support consultancy services, which include security management, content management, wireless applications and IP communications, OSS design authority, and other managed services to global telecommunication providers, systems integrators, outsource providers, and vendors;

and unified communications and voice solutions.." Businessweek

In addition, he was responsible for worldwide business development for Motorola's Cellular Infrastructure Group. During his tenure at Motorola, where he served in a variety of capacities, Mr. Douglas developed new businesses in wireless data services and IP professional services.
