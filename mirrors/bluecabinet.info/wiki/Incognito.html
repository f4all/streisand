Background

Incognito Software is a global provider of broadband software provisioning and activation solutions that help monetize IP based subscriber and commercial services. Service providers and large enterprises worldwide use our products to quickly and cost-effectively deploy data, voice, and video services across a multitude of networks.

Leading broadband providers like Insight Communications, Sprint, Qwest, UK's BT Group and Ministry of Defence, Hong Kong's I-Cable, Chile's VTR GlobalCom, Brazil's NET and Singapore's StarHub are already using products from Incognito Software to realize new revenues from the "triple-play" - voice, video, and data services.

The management team at Incognito Software is active in many industry organizations such as the Canadian Steering Committee on Numbering (CSCN) for ENUM (TElephone NUmber Mapping) resources, Internet Engineering Task Force (IETF), Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN), Society of Cable Telecommunications Engineers (SCTE), CableLabs, and DSL Forum. They regularly participate in defining new Internet protocols and standards such as ENUM and SIP (Session Initiation Protocol). For instance, in response to the "VoIP Peering Request for Information" issued by CableLabs in December 2005, Incognito Software addressed the management of ENUM resource records under a cable operator's control. Products

  • Broadband Command Center

Automated provisioning for DOCSIS, TR-069, PacketCable, and SIP devices.

Broadband Command Center with Address Commander integrates to become an efficient law enforcement reporting solution to help you comply with law enforcement agencies or CALEA requirements. This solution enables swift reporting on any device or IP address for both current and historical leases.[2]

  • Address Commander

Service provider–grade IPAM for IPv4 and IPv6 address management.

Easily generate comprehensive reports detailling your IP address allocation for the purposes of planning, RIR reconciliation, or lawful enforcement requirements.

  • Name Commander

Large-scale DNS management for BIND and controlled name servers.

  • Bandwidth Activity Reporter

Bandwidth usage intelligence for broadband network management.

  • Service Activation Center

Centralized and automatic service activation and management.

  • Auto Configuration Server

End-to-end TR-069 service fulfillment and device provisioning solution.

Management Team

Name Title Description
Stephane Bourque Blog CEO and President Stephane Bourque is the technological inspiration behind Incognito Software's provisioning solutions.

As CEO, Stephane has built an elite team of dedicated engineers and championed Incognito's development of high performance, multi-platform IP and DNS software.

Originally from Montreal, Canada, and educated at Concordia University, Stephane applied his computer engineering background at Banyan Systems to design enterprise network management systems for Fortune 1000 companies like Bell Canada.

Chris Busch Blog Chief Technology Officer As Incognito CTO, Chris Busch drives IP technology investment and direction for Incognito Software. He brings over 14 years experience in broadband markets including cable, wireline, wireless, and satellite.

Previously with Nortel Networks as Senior Technology Advisor to the CTO of Cable MSO Global Solutions, Chris has also led teams in terabit IP core routing, BRAS IP services, wireless IP access, wireline and cable MSO access, video on demand, and triple-play IP video solutions. Chris draws on a wealth of knowledge in cable MSO video and IP data networking that are key to both CableLabs and Broadband Forum standards for subscriber service and device management technologies.

Will Yan Senior VP, Worldwide Sales A veteran of the technology industry, Will brings close to 20 years of experience in executive management, business development, strategy, and sales management. At Incognito, he is responsible for setting sales strategic directions, establishing sales process, building sales support tools such as pricing models, ROI models and marketing collaterals and orchestrating cross-functional teams with Product Management, Support and Engineering.

Previously, Will was the Chief Market Strategist for the Americas Cable Market at Alott Communications. He has also served in the role of Executive Vice President, Sales, Marketing and Business Development at Auspice, a platform and OSS/network management software vendor sold to Arris. He was also the Director of Business Development in the CRM and Business intelligence group of Oracle Corporation.

Wayne Haveling Customer Care Manager Wayne created the Technical Support department for Incognito, designed a comprehensive support database, and developed solutions for customers in all markets including broadband, enterprise, and government. Dan Strini QA Manager As QA Manager, Daniel Strini works with a team of QA engineers and specialists to ensure complete testing and verification of Incognito's software product lines.
