Background

SeaNet Technologies is a private company.

"SeaNet Technologies, Inc. provides application performance measurement and reporting solutions for complex and multi-tiered enterprise applications. The company’s products are used in end to end performance measurement, data collection, diagnosing protocol or application tiers, multi-tier message aggregation, and in understanding the business context. It serves customers in the enterprise and financial services industries. The company was founded in 2001 and is based in New York, New York with additional offices in Houston, Texas; San Jose, California; Connecticut; and New Jersey." Businessweek

Products

SeaNet Modules page


SeaView/RTM (Real-Time Monitor)SeaView/RTM (Real-Time Monitor) probes provide source information that is processed by all other components of the SeaView system. Probes passively read network traffic from the wire, decode the network packets, and extract their logical, application, and business messages. SeaNet deploys intelligent software called the RTM Engine within the probe that decodes the packet at line speed 24X7. This pre-processing is one of the advantages of the SeaNet design and provides a very scalable and extremely fast processing tool. This architecture supports capture and processing of over 400,000 messages per second and is in production today.


SeaView/Matcher reads RTM events sent by RTM Probes, assembles messages that make up logical business transactions, and creates records corresponding to complete logical business transactions with the timestamps for each event that made up the transaction. Matcher also summarizes the data in real time based on specified configuration criteria and sends summary and detail data to several other components, including SeaView/Central. It also interfaces with SeaView/ PDB, and/or optionally to PDB/HS for high volume, real time detail data storage and query.


SeaView/Central is the console that controls the RTM probes. It consolidates and stores real-time data from the distributed RTM appliances and performs deterministic correlation and matching. The SeaView State Engines are located in this component.


SeaView/PDB (Performance Data Base) assembles all transaction analysis into a historical repository for detailed historical analysis.


SeaView PDB/High Speed is an optional component that enables storage of extremely large amounts of detailed transaction data in real-time. The component is based on the use of parallelism, which is specifically designed to load massive amounts of data at high speeds (up to 2GB per minute), query large databases of billions of rows, and respond to such queries in seconds.


SeaView/VAS (Visual Analysis Server) makes real-time and historical data available for OLAP analysis by SeaView/VAC, as well as the HTML Dashboard.


SeaView/VAC (Visual Analysis Console) makes detailed visualization and multi-dimensional analysis possible on both the real-time monitoring of RTM or the historical data-store of PDB.


SeaView Suite Provides a real-time comprehensive data analysis system that enables complete measurement and analysis. The SeaView solution suite includes SeaView/RTM, which collects transaction data."

People Responsible

Board Members

    • Raymond Marra, CEO and President

Mr. Marra has over 25 years of experience generating significant new sources of revenue for many of the world's most prominent technology firms. He has created and led numerous successful business development efforts across a large number of industries both domestically and internationally.

Mr. Marra served as Vice President of Worldwide Alliances at Riversoft Inc. (IBM) where under his leadership the restructured sales division achieved results equaling 40% of overall company sales in less than one year.

As Vice President of OEM Sales at Micromuse (IBM)' Mr. Marra relaunched the Federal Sales division successfully positioning Netcool as a Best of Breed solution, with many of the leading systems integrators adopting the technology resulting in significant revenue attainment.

Prior to Micromuse, Mr. Marra launched the Channel Sales division at SMARTS Inc. (EMC) as their first Vice President in this role. Mr. Marra was also an integral part of the original team that founded Objective Systems Integrators Inc. serving in many roles including Director of Channel Sales.


    • Bernie Davidovics, Founder/CTO

Mr. Davidovics is one of the world’s preeminent experts in the field of application performance management. His 30 year career has been spent working with Wall St. and Fortune 500 firms developing solutions for capacity planning and network/application performance management.

Prior to founding SeaNet in 2001, he was the global head of Performance Management for Predictive Systems (NASD:PRDS), a leading network infrastructure services firm. At Predictive, he pioneered solutions for network performance management, usage-based billing, and capacity planning in the most rigorous Wall St. and Telecom service provider environments.

Prior to Predictive, Mr. Davidovics was a consultant to numerous Fortune 500 companies on performance management issues in their mission applications. Mr. Davidovics began his career on Wall St. at Chase, and later moved to Legent Corporation (NASD:LGNT – acquired by Computer Associates for $1.2B), one largest software companies focused on the management of enterprise-wide computer systems.

Mr. Davidovics has published numerous papers and performs speaking engagements on systems management, performance management and capacity planning.
