Background

"SevOne, Inc. is venture funded and backed by a group of private investors, each with long and successful backgrounds in business and technology."

"SevOne Inc. provides a network performance management solution that delivers fast, scalable, and comprehensive real-time monitoring, troubleshooting and performance reporting solution to ensure application performance and reliability. The SevOne Performance Appliance Solution (SevOne PAS) and SevOne Distributed NetFlow Collector (SevOne DNC) enable IT organizations to keep increasingly complex networks, servers and applications functioning at peak performance levels." CrunchBase

Products

  • SevOne Dedicated NetFlow Collector (DNC) - take full advantage of valuable Flow data for use in network and application performance management. Our DNC leverages the same architecture and design principles used in other SevOne appliances to create the industry’s most comprehensive, scalable and affordable solution for Flow collection and reporting.
  • SevOne Cluster™ technology allows you to grow your data collection infinitely.

Unique Capabilities:

Complete and Instant Visibility

Enables visibility into legacy networks, virtualized environments, cloud networks, voice/data/video networks and flow data — all from a single, unified console and reporting engine.

Immediate access to both real-time data as well as up to a year of raw data allows engineers to drill into the past with the same granularity as the present.

Accuracy-at-Scale Collection

300 polling threads per appliance ensures that data is collected at the scheduled times and that no gaps in visibility occur as collection needs scale.

Speed-at-Scale Reporting

Eliminates the scalability bottleneck of a centralized reporting server by utilizing the reporting capabilities of all peered appliances, resulting in network-wide reports that take seconds, not hours.

Proven "Big Data" Deployments

Numerous customers that manage millions of network elements and run baseline analytics in the trillions have access to the results in a single easy-to-use web interface

Return On Investment (ROI)

The “All-in-One” appliance (collection, storage and reporting) and its powerful capabilities involve a much smaller solution footprint, need less daily management, and are easy to use — resulting in improvements in CAPEX/OPEX expenses.

Board of Directors

SevOne is guided by an influential board of directors.

John Ryan For 15 years, John Ryan has been a private investor and active director of early stage technology companies. The founder of SunGard Data Systems, Inc., a formerly publicly-held computer services company that was recently taken private, Mr. Ryan served as its Chairman and CEO from 1976 to 1987. Since then, he has been a principal in Devon Hill Ventures, a venture investing and consulting firm with a strong technology portfolio.

Mr. Ryan has directed 25 private and public information technology companies where Devon Hill Ventures has invested. Besides serving on SevOne's board today, Mr. Ryan is currently a director of several technology and Internet companies. Among them are SAQARRA, Neoware Systems and eResearch Technology.

Mr. Ryan has a BS degree from Yale University and an MBA from Harvard Business School.


Robert S. Adelson — Chairman Bob founded Osage Investments in 1990 and has built a track record as a top-performing venture investor, consistently delivering top-decile returns on his venture investments. He is a native of Tulsa, Oklahoma, where he is a partner in a fourth generation family business. Prior to founding Osage, Bob clerked with the honorable Walter K. Stapleton, U.S. Third Circuit Court of Appeals, and practiced corporate law at the Philadelphia law firm of Wolf, Block, Schorr & Solis-Cohen. Bob's previous venture investments include Zany Brainy, Inphonic (NASDAQ: INPC), Internet Capital Group (NASDAQ: ICGE), and GHR Systems (acquired by Metavante). He is a graduate of The Yale Law School and of Yale College. Bob is a trustee of Jefferson University Hospital, where he currently serves as Chairman of the Board, Jefferson University, and Jefferson Health System. He is also President of the Board of Trustees of The Philadelphia School and is a member of The Yale Law School Fund.

He serves on the Board of Directors of Melior Discovery, SevOne, and Redlasso, and is Chairman of Melior Discovery.

Michael Phelan Michael Phelan has served as President and CEO of SevOne since September 2006 and has led the company’s founding team. As a seasoned executive and entrepreneur in the storage networking industry, Mr. Phelan has served successfully in many leadership roles including Founder/President of StorNet, Inc. and CEO/COO of SoluNet, Inc.

Mr. Phelan founded StorNet in 1990 and as the company’s CEO, he successfully built the company into a leading national consulting and storage networking integration firm. StorNet enjoyed 11 years of consecutive double-digit growth and profitability and achieved $80 million in revenue. Mr. Phelan’s leadership and expertise led the company to being awarded the Emerging Business of the Year Award by Ernst and Young in 1994. The company was sold to Vangard Technologies, Inc. in 2000. In addition, Mr. Phelan was named one of the Top 20 Visionaries in the storage networking market by CMS VAR Business magazine in 2001.

Vess Bakalov As one of the co-founders of SevOne, Vess Bakalov serves as Chief Technology Officer and is responsible for leading the company’s product development and design, and overall vision of the technology architecture.

Prior to SevOne, Mr. Bakalov served in a number of senior technical positions at BankOne, including Assistant Vice President and Network Architect. He also led the network management team for the Credit Card Services division of the company, which was the largest issuer of VISA credit cards in the U.S. During his time at BankOne, Mr. Bakalov also taught undergraduate and graduate courses as an Adjunct Professor of Computer Science at the University of Delaware.

Mr. Bakalov began his career as a technical advisor for a fast-growing CLEC. There, he led the design and engineering effort behind that company's first DSL deployment. He was also in charge of architecting and developing the network management and instrumentation strategy for the firm.

Investors

"SevOne, Inc. is venture funded and backed by a group of private investors, each with long and successful backgrounds in business and technology.

In July 2007, Osage Ventures led a Series A Preferred placement in SevOne. Osage is a venture capital fund that invests in entrepreneurial companies with a unique concept or product. Osage provides capital and value-added services to technology and life science businesses in the Northeast corridor. Robert S. Adelson is President of Philadelphia-based Osage Investments and serves on SevOne's board of directors.

In addition to the Osage Series A investment, SevOne, Inc. is backed by a private investment group led by SevOne CEO and Co-Founder Michael Phelan, who is a seasoned executive/entrepreneur in the storage and networking industries. Mr. Phelan founded StorNet in 1990 and built the company as CEO into a leading national consulting and storage networking integration firm.

In March, 2009, Osage Ventures led a second round of financing in SevOne along with several private investors.

Other private investors in SevOne include Jonathan Brassington, the CEO and Founding Partner of Liquid Hub, Inc.; Attorney Lawrence Phelan, a Principal of Phelan, Hallinan & Schmieg, LLP law firm; Adrian Stone, Founder of SurePlan International; and John Ryan, a SevOne board member and director of many technology companies." - SevOne -
