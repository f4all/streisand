Products

Broadband Analysis and Monitoring, Interception

It offers GPON Monitor, a centralized performance analyzer and monitor for GPON networks.

The company also provides testing and monitoring products, such as

  • DOCSIS Xpert, a multi-layer DOCSIS analyzer Brochure
  • DSL Xpert, a multi-layer ADSL analyzer Brochure
  • GPON Xpert, a multi-layer GPON analyzer in portable and lab versions Brochure
  • VDSL Xpert, a non-intrusive analyzer for VDSL2 lines. Brochure


In addition, it offers interception products, including

  • DSL Phantom, a passive ADSL tapping device that monitors and records data transparently within the ADSL physical layer.DSL Phantom "Lawful Interception" Photo of the Phantom Brochure pdf in English
  • VDSL Phantom, a passive tapping device for lawful interception (LI) of VDSL2 lines Brochure pdf in English
  • GPON Phantom, a passive monitoring solution that records and stores broadband digital information on fiber lines using GPON technology Brochure
  • DOCSIS Phantom, a passive monitoring solution that records and stores broadband digital information on cable networks, and deploys either in the head-end or near the target cable modem Brochure
  • Phantom Viewer, a monitoring application offering comprehensive displays of network traffic, including Internet browsing, Email services, FTP, instant messaging, and voice calls. Brochure

Further, the company provides technical support services.
