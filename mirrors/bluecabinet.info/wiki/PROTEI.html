Background

PROTEI is one of Russia´s leading providers of IT and telecom solutions. The company was founded in 1997, and now has an elite team of 150 professionals located in Russian headquarter (including R&D center) and MENA regional office in Jordon. More than 34 mobile and fixed-line operators in 19 countries use PROTEI solutions to serve over 70 million subscribers.

PROTEI portfolio includes VAS and messaging platforms, Roaming solutions, NGN equipment, DPI/PCRF platform.
