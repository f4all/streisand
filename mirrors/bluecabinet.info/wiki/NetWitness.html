Background

Netwitness is owned by RSA a division of EMC2.

see Blue cabinet/RSA

  • 4.11: EMC acquires NetWitness, combines with RSA: With the acquisition, which closed Friday, EMC will delve deeper into network security monitoring and analysis.CNET

Products

NetWitness provides solutions for full data capture and traffic recording. It was bought by RSA in April 2011. It can monitor every e-mail and voice call passing through a point of interception. The device can be installed at a POP (Point of Presence) in an ISP, according to its configuration guides.
