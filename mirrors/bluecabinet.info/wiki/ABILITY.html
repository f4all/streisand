Background

ABILITY is privately held by Anatoly Hurgin.

Rony Reshef is in charge of Sales and Marketing. There are 11-50 employees.

"ABILITY was founded in 1993 by a team of experts in military intelligence and communications who were joined by specialists in electronics and mathematics. Their goal was simple but extraordinarily complex – to devise state of the art interception and decryption solutions that would serve the needs and ever-increasing challenges of security and intelligence agencies, military forces, police and homeland security services around the world." ABILITY

Manufacturers of stand-alone, integrated and custom technological devices of various sizes for the decryption and interception of communications -- for the purpose of gathering intelligence (data-mining) and surveillance.

Products

ABILITY products page, includes links to operating manuals.


ABILITY surveillance devices are used for both Cellular Interception and Satellite Interception.

Cellular Interception devices i.e. 3G-Cat have been created specifically to be used with 3G devices. The 3G devices come from the "3" brand that is a venture partnership between INVESTOR AB (Sweden) and Huctchinson-Whampoa Ltd.(HWL) (Hong Kong) and also included Ericsson (Sweden) in their partnership to create imGO to expand their business in telecommunications and internet technologies.

Ericsson has contracted with Zain to provide 3G mobile in North Africa and the Middle East.

GSM Interceptors

IBIS II is restricted to the public and is only sold to Law Enforcement Agencies and Authorized Governmental Agencies as surveillance tool to intercept and track GSM communication of all types. Advertised as the most advanced system available that has the ability of integrating all relevant subsystems in a single unit that allows the user to "scan, analyze, intercept, monitor, record and track GSM mobiles, regardless if they are encrypted by A5.1 or A5.2 encryption (monitoring is not done by forcing the mobile to use A5.0 or A5.2 but rather by an integrated deciphering capability)." The IBIS–II is a stand-alone, not requiring notification or cooperation between the user and network provider.

Additional Active and Passive GSM Cellular Interception Products:

  • GTReS – GSM Traffic Recording System - Passive GSM Interceptor
  • IBIS II – In-Between Interception System] - Active GSM Interceptor
  • IICat – IMSI/IMEI Catcher - Active GSM IMSI/IMEI catcher
  • 3G-Cat - Long-awaited solution for 3G (UMTS) networks
  • operating manual
  • ACIS – Advanced CDMA Intercepted System - Passive CDMA Interceptor

Satellite Interception

Satellite interception for major mobile satellite communication systems: IRIDIUM, THURAYA and for Satellite Link Interception System (SLIS) for the interception of global and regional satellite communication operators (“Intelsat”, “Eutelsat”, “Arabsat”, “Domsat”, “Indosat”, etc.) including VSAT and DCME.
