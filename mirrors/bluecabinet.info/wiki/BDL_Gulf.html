Background

"With revenues approaching the US$300m mark, Saudi Arabia-based BDL features among the region’s broadline elite and appears in The Power List by virtue of the fact it now operates a subsidiary in Dubai.

BDL Gulf has come a long way in the year or so since its launch under ex-Acer channel chief Sunil Nair, signing a slew of partnerships, enamouring itself to the both the commercial and retail channels, and unveiling in-country operations in Kuwait." Arabian Business Channel Power List

"BDL, established in the year 1998 in Saudi Arabia, is a leading name in the IT distribution business in the Middle East and North Africa. We have enhanced our business all over Middle East and Egypt within a span of few years. With extensive infrastructure and over 300 qualified professionals, BDL is growing rapidly every year. BDL represents major Brands in Computers, Printers, Peripherals, software and Consumer Electronics businesses. Our focus is to excel in rendering efficient distribution, marketing and services to our partners. BDL has received numerous awards from leading brands such as Acer, Lenovo, Lexmark, MSI etc.

BDL has 6 strategically located warehousing facilities, comprising more than 34,000 sq. ft, in Riyadh, Jeddah, Khobar (Saudi Arabia),Cairo (Egypt),and Dubai serving more than 3000 resellers. BDL is very well positioned to capture a respectable market share in the Middle East and Africa's IT market.

VISION To be the leading provider of IT solutions by every measurement.

MISSION BDL will endeavor to help its partners grow by focussing on broadline distribution and delivering superior value and services and interacting effectively.

VALUES We commit to these values to guide our decisions and our behaviors.

We meet and exceed customer expectations. We keep our promises.We involve our people, continuously train and educate them and share a common team spirit.We continuously improve our processes, products and services based on feedback and newest technologies.We value long term relationships and open communications with our customers, suppliers and partners." JD.AE

BDL Gulf is a subsidiary of BDL Trading Establishment

"BDL Trading Establishment distributes information technology products in Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates, and Egypt. It supplies computers, printers, peripherals, software, and consumer electronics of various brands. The company also offers network solutions, including design, planning, implementation, installation, management, and servicing networks. In addition, it repairs broken or unstable networks. The company supplies products to system builders and integrators, direct market retailers and etailers, computer and government entities, educational institutions, and hypermarkets and bookstores. It has locations in Riyadh, Jeddah, and Alkhobar in Saudi Arabia; Cairo, Egypt; and Dubai, the United Arab Emirates. BDL Trading Establishment was founded in 1998 and is based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia." Businessweek
