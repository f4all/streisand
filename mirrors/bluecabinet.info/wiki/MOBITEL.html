"MOBITEL works as a mobile phones wholesale distributor since 1998. MOBITEL is a direct distributor for such producers as Philips, Nokia, Samsung and others. MOBITEL cooperates with such major operators as T-Mobile, Telefonica, MTS/UMC.

With its own car fleet and warehouses MOBITEL acts as a logistics operator for third parties.

  • MOBITEL wholesale services: arranging production and delivery of handsets under mobile operators' brands from original equipment manufacturers (OEM) and original design manufacturers (ODM) direct delivery from producers consolidated deliveries for operators and distributors
  • MOBITEL logistics services : delivery of handsets and SIMs import clearance, warehouse services, product adaptation including packing & unpacking.

Our services include:

  • delivery of goods from a manufacturer/seller to customers either by our own transport

or by finding a third partner;

  • providing warehousing facilities, either own or third party’s; inspecting goods.

Our partners are TNT Logistic, Czech Air Lines, Aeroflot, Schenker, LOT, AEROSVIT
