Products

Services

"The company offers digital forensics and incident response services, such as digital evidence acquisition, analysis, and containment services; and malicious code analysis and reverse engineering services, which comprise assembly language analysis.

It also provides IT continuity planning, infrastructure optimization, secure hosting of IT infrastructure and applications, product development, and research in software and hardware security.

In addition, the company offers network risk assessment and threat monitoring services, including

  • footprint analysis,
  • security assessment, and
  • threat monitoring solutions, as well as
  • network configuration,
  • patch schedule, and
  • antivirus update assessment services; and
  • security research services, such as software vulnerability discovery,
  • automation of software test and analysis, and
  • computer network defense services."
