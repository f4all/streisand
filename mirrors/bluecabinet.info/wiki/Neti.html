Background

English webpage of NETI

The company’s sole proprietor is the Theodore Puskás Foundation (PTA), a non-profit public benefit entity supervised by the e-Governmental Centre of the Prime Minister’s Office. Its objective is the advancement of modern technologies through transfer, consulting and technology audit. PTA’s main focus of activities are directed at the security-related aspects of Information and Communication Technologies. PTA is host to CERT-Hungary, the Network-, and Cyber-security Centre of the Government.

Products

Monitoring, Telephone tapping, Analytical Tools, Tactical Appliances. Services are aimed for law enforcement.

BONGO Neti's primary product. BONGO is a framework with a wide span of features like monitoring, DPI and telephone tapping.

Netimon Netimon is a system for tapping mobile and landline telephones for law enforcement agencies.

Netiphone Netiphone is an encryption system for mobile phones with the possibility of interception for central authorities in the network.
