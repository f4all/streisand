People Responsible

Investors

  • Hazem Ben-Gacem Photo

Hazem Ben-Gacem is Co-Head of Investcorp Technology Partners. Hazem joined Investcorp in 1994 as a member of the European corporate investment team. Hazem is a founding member of the group and is currently a board director of eviivo, kgb, Utimaco Safeware AG, Skrill, OpSec Security Group and TDX Group. Prior to joining Investcorp, Hazem was a member of Credit Suisse First Boston's mergers and acquisitions team in New York.

Hazem holds a BA (Hons) in Economics from Harvard University. He also serves on the Dean's Council for the Harvard Kennedy School of Government.
