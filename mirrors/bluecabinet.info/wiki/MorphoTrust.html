Background

History

As of July, 2011 MorphoTrust USA, Inc. operates as a subsidiary of Safran SA.

MorphoTrust USA, Inc. was formerly known as L-1 Identity Solutions Inc. and changed its name in 2011.

The company also has a facial recognition solution in use by the FBI for their Next Generation Identity program as well as mtiple techologies in use at the Department of Defense. [1]

MorphoTrust USA offers "the largest network of service centers in the U.S., with locations across all 50 states, who can also facilitate quick, convenient ways to manage many identity-related tasks. These centers enable quick and efficient enrollment and business services including the opportunity to outsource certain operations for state agencies. Over 2.5 million individuals will be served through the MorphoTrust centers in 2012, with that number steadily growing. In addition, we are an authorized service provider for the Federal Government including FBI channeling, and Aviation channeling, as well as for state, local, and commercial entities that certify trusted professionals or vet applicants in industries including education, child, senior and health care, law enforcement and financial services." [2]


According to BusinessWeek,"MorphoTrust USA, Inc. provides identity solutions.

  • It offers secure ID issuance solutions;
  • enrollment solutions; and identity management solutions, which include biometric solutions and devices; document authentication, scanning, and archiving solutions and devices;
  • knowledge and skills testing solutions; criminal processing solutions and devices; and commercial solutions, such as critical infrastructure solutions.

The company also provides identity management practices, such as DMV public private partnerships, DMV system modernization, and verified enrollment and identity management platforms and solutions.

In addition, it offers transaction solutions, which include biometric screening solutions and devices;

  • border management solutions and devices;
  • self-service and kiosk solutions; document authentication, scanning, and archiving solutions and devices;
  • and online/mobile solutions.

Further, the company provides investigative solutions, such as investigative and forensic solutions, and crime prevention solutions.

  • It serves government-issued ID segments, such as driver licenses and passports;
  • border management, public safety, law enforcement, aviation, retail, banking, and employee/applicant vetting markets."
