Owner President: Metrocom Inc

July 1999 – January 2007 (7 years 7 months) Founded Metrocom to provide telecommunications services and wholesale international routes to global carriers. Established alliances with PTT’s and carrier companies to expand long distance network penetration. Preparation of business plans and financial models to increase company strength. Design and deployment of Cisco Voice over IP networks into foreign countries for connection to Global based wholesale telecom companies. Prepared network requirements, cost analysis / benefits for all company systems integration. Responsible for contract negotiations and vendor selection. Development of new markets and potential partnerships in South and Central America, Europe and Middle East. Products and services included prepaid telecom services. Metrocom assisted in the design and development of the WiFi network for Inversiones Veserteca in Venezuela for the PDVSA communications network. Metrocom also developed a WiMAX project for the Republic of Honduras.

Investors Major Shareholders

The Company was founded by the largest foreign investors: Sonera (Finland), Telia International AB (Sweden) now merged into TeliaSonera, along with Telenor Invest AS (Norway). Telecom equipment was purchased from Nokia.

The owners of the Company as of March 2011 are as follows: 

   TeliaSonera – owns 35.6% shares directly and 8.2% through Telecominvest;
   AF Telecom (owner – Alisher Usmanov) owns - 8% shares directly and 23.1% through Telecominvest;
   Altimo (owner – Alfa Group) owns - 25.1%.


Jeffrey Galmond, a Danish lawyer publicly claims to own and control Telecominvest, but it is rumored to in fact represent the personal interests of former Russian telecommunications minister Leonid Reiman.[65]

A battle for control of the company is ongoing between Alfa Group and Jeffrey Galmond's party.

Unlike other companies from the Big Three, MegaFon is not a public company, therefore it does not have to disclose all the information on the Company’s business (for example: it does not publish quarterly reports of the issuer of securities).
