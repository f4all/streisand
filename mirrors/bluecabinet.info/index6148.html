Background

Prolexic has a global, cloud-based network protection service that includes staff to mitigate and counter DDoS attacks, including using forensics.

"typical DDoS mitigation time is just 1-5 minutes after traffic starts flowing through Prolexic’s scrubbing centers. The "network of DDoS scrubbing centers are located in London, Hong Kong, San Jose, California and Ashburn, Virginia"

"Our DDoS scrubbing centers are supported by four Tier 1 global telecommunications carriers. Unlike other DDoS mitigation providers, we don’t piggyback other services such as DNS on our DDoS mitigation network. So what does this mean? It means we can mitigate the largest DDoS attacks launched in the world – the high packet-per-second DDoS floods that bring down sites, and low and slow encrypted Layer 7 attacks that cripple applications. And this is really important – because of this immense anti-DDoS bandwidth, it means that we can provide DDoS protection services for multiple clients and fight multiple DDoS attacks at once.

When a DDoS attack is detected, our DDoS protection services are implemented within minutes. Upon activation of DDoS protection, a Prolexic customer routes in-bound traffic to the nearest Prolexic scrubbing center, where proprietary DDoS filtering techniques, advanced routing, and patent-pending anti-DoS hardware devices remove DDoS traffic close to the source of the botnet activity. Clean traffic is then routed back to the customer’s network.

Because we dedicate more bandwidth to DDoS denial of service attack traffic than any other provider – supplemented by proprietary tools, techniques, and experienced security experts – we can protect your site(s) against even the largest and most complex DDoS attacks. Where other DDoS mitigation providers rely on 3 or 4 technologies and anti-DDoS techniques to monitor and mitigate denial of service attacks, Prolexic uses over 20 DDoS mitigation technologies – many of them proprietary.

Prolexic mitigates every type of DDoS attack at every layer including Layer 3, 4, and 7. We even have a proven solution against encrypted attacks that vandalize HTTPS traffic in real time. Further, we use certified FIPS-140-2 Level 3 key management encryption tools with passive SSL decryption for extremely high performance. Often DDoS attackers who see traffic has been re-routed through our DDoS mitigation network immediately abandon their attacks – they know they do not have the capacity to overcome Prolexic." People Responsible

Board Members

Scott Hammack, CEO

Mr. Hammack joined Prolexic in 2011 and brings a wealth of experience managing and growing start-up and publicly held companies. Most recently he was CEO of e-dmz, which was acquired by Quest Software in 2011.

Previously, he has served as CEO of Cyberguard Corporation, which was acquired by Secure Computing in 2004, and CEO of MasterChart, Inc., which was purchased by Allscripts in 2001.

He is a graduate of Northwestern University with a degree in biomedical engineering.


Jeanne Prayther, CFO

Ms. Prayther, a Certified Public Accountant (CPA), joined Prolexic in 2012 and brings more than 25 years of experience in the corporate accounting field.

Most recently, she was the CFO for Maritime Telecommunications Network. She has also held executive accounting positions at various public companies including Levitt Corporation, Mastec, and Daleen Technologies.

Previously, she worked at KPMG LLP for 12 years serving audit clients in the Boston and Miami offices.

She graduated from Bentley College in Waltham, Massachusetts


Neal Quinn, COO

Mr. Quinn brings 14 years of experience in networking, Internet backbone design, and managed services to Prolexic. Prior to joining Prolexic in 2008, he launched his own consulting firm serving businesses and public sector teams in the U.K. and U.S and before that, he served as network architect for Globix Corporation.

He has a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Physics from Johns Hopkins University and is a Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert


Louis Suchy, VP of Research and Development

Mr. Suchy has more than 10 years of experience in software architecture and technology engineering. Prior to Prolexic, he was executive director of Development for Kaplan Higher Education.

He has a degree in Information Systems Management and an MBA from Kaplan University.


Stuart Scholly, President

Mr. Scholly joined Prolexic in 2011 and brings more than 20 years of technology and product management experience. Prior to joining Prolexic, he was chief operating officer at Physicians Interactive. He has also held senior leadership positions in product management and business development at Allscripts.

He won the 2010 Spotlight Award from the Illinois Technology Association, awarded to the CTO/CIO for leadership and outstanding contributions in the application of information technology.

He received an MBA from the Kellogg School of Management and a Bachelors of Science Degree in Economics and English from the University of Wisconsin – Madison.


Greg Burns, Vice President of Sales Operations

Mr. Burns joined Prolexic in September 2008. Previously he held senior management positions in service and technology-related businesses, including Rain Bird Corporation where he was responsible for starting and growing a new business division. He has a degree in Engineering from Lafayette College and an MBA from The University of Notre Dame


Rudy Cifolelli, Senior VP, Sales

Mr. Cifolelli has more than 20 years of sales and management experience with high-tech companies. Prior to Prolexic, he was with McAfee and held several sales and executive positions including VP North American Data Protection Sales. Prior to McAfee, he was president of SafeBoot Corporation which was sold to McAfee in 2007.

He is a graduate of Ferris State University and holds a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Information Systems


Michael E. Donner, Senior VP, Chief Marketing Officer

Mr. Donner has more than 20 years of marketing, inside sales and demand generation experience with high-tech companies. Prior to engaging with Prolexic, he ran a consulting company and served as the interim head of marketing for Thales e-Security, Tech Data and IntelliDOT Corporation.

He has also held executive roles at Capgemini, Eclipsys Corporation and Telelogic AB.

Mr. Donner has a Bachelor of Science Degree in Marketing, Management and International Business from Babson College
