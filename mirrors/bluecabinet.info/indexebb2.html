Background

As of October 9, 2008, Edge Intelligence, LLC operates as a subsidiary of Abraxas Corporation.

"Edge Intelligence, LLC provides solutions to intelligence, law enforcement, and commercial sectors."

"The EDGE R&D was formed as a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) back in May of 2000 in El Paso, Texas. We started as a small team of Subject Matter Experts (SME) training Patriot Battalions in engagement and force protection operations at Fort Bliss, Texas. Over the years we have expanded into many diverse areas and now have contracts throughout the United States." crunchbase

Products

  • Advanced Digital Evidence Processing Technology (ADEPT) system, a suite of tools that handle various aspects of the investigative/analytical process.
  • ADEPT Core, a database, evidence repository, and data mining engine that supports the system.
  • ADEPT Investigator, an application for review, search, and analysis of relevant information from huge evidences
  • ADEPT Op Center, a multi-user and multi-agency environment that enables collaboration through Internet.

Key Executives

Richard Hollis Helms, CEO

Mr. Helms is a Founder and CEO of Abraxas Engineering and Abraxas Corporation.

Teddy Lindsey, Founder

Mr.Teddy Lindsey Founded Edge Research and Development (Alternatively, Edge Intelligence).

Mr. Lindsey served as the Vice President of Research and Development at Abraxas Corporation. He is a respected and accomplished Computer Scientist. Mr. Lindsey is charged with expanding Abraxas product offering and accelerating the delivery of new cyber solutions. He leads Abraxas efforts in the area of advanced and innovative technology products across the entire Abraxas customer suite.

Mr. Lindsey has created a dozen products across a variety of disciplines ranging from incident response and forensic tools to investigative analysis and tactical command systems.

Prior to joining Abraxas Corporation, Mr. Lindsey served as Director of Research for the Microsoft and Qualcomm joint venture, Wireless Knowledge, Inc., served as the Chief Architect and lead Engineer on several commercial software products and finally a Computer Scientist for the FBI.

He is a frequent speaker at national law enforcement, computer security and counterterrorism conferences.

Mr. Lindsey holds a B.S. in Computer Information Systems from San Diego State University.
