Investor AB is headquartered in Stockholm, Sweden

INVESTOR AB was founded by the Wallenberg family of Sweden, known for their long history in banking, military complex industries (see Saab Group, Bofar, ABB, Electrolux,______), telecommunications (see Ericsson)and more recently pharmaceuticals and healthcare (see AstraZeneca, MÃ¶lnlycke Health Care, Aleris, wireless/mobile communications (see "3")and other internet technologies (see "imGO"), including ABB technology investments, Rukus )and through Investor Capital Partners - Asia Fund, L.P., along with their wholly-owned subsidiary Investor Growth Capital, including the US division that invests in Technology and Healthcare. and investments in the independent EQT funds. The Wallenberg family also acquires and holds company stock shares through FAM, Providia and Patricia...

"Investor AB, Sweden's largest holding company with total net asset value close to US$20 billion, is a leading shareholder in a number of global companies [over 100], including ABB, AstraZeneca, Ericsson, Electrolux, OM Gruppen, Saab, Scania, SEB and StoraEnso. In addition, through its New Investments arm, Investor has made a number of promising investments in early stage and growth companies in the United States, Asia and throughout Europe. Investor is focused on identifying emerging leaders in high growth sectors such as technology and health care. Investor Capital Partners - Asia Fund, L.P. is the investment vehicle of Investor AB in Asia and focuses on buy-outs and providing growth capital."</ref/http://203.129.67.141/upload_docs/2000/05/Corporate/77/77_eng.htm> Additionally, Investor AB has subsidaries and finanacial investments in companies that are private and publicly held.

Investor AB is a business partner with Henderson-Whampoa Limited (HWL) for the international mobile phone provider "3" brand. In addition, Investor AB, Ericsson and HWL have partnered to create the investment company "imGO" to expand "its business to focus on the mobile Internet industry in Asia and develop a significant presence in the wireless Internet industry. The Company will invest in telecommunication-related technologies, wireless Internet enabling technologies, software applications, hardware (devices and terminals), wireless content providers and broadband infrastructure."

"The three shareholders have contributed a total of US$177 million to the Company. Investor is the lead shareholder with 33.7 percent of the enlarged share capital. Ericsson holds 16.4 percent and Hutchison, 9.9 percent. The existing shareholders Guoco Group, a member of the Hong Leong Group Malaysia, holds 24.2 percent of the enlarged share capital. A substantial portion of Investor's stake in the Company is earmarked for the Investor Capital Partners - Asia Fund, L.P."


The Investor Capital Partners - Asia Fund, L.P. is represented by Orrick aka "The China Team", which represents Carlyle Venture Funds among others.
