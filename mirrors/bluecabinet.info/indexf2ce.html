Background

"Akorn Telecommunications provides a full range of telecoms, civil works and electromechanical services to a wide range of vendors and operators throughout Europe, Middle East, Gulf, Far East, and Africa.

Akorn also provides resources being individual experts as well as full Turnkey Projects.

With Project Managers, Supervisors and Coordinators to lead its teams of engineers, Akorn is able to provide support to Mobile and Fixed Networks, Civil Works and Power Systems in the following fields:

• Turnkey Implementation Services for 2G, 3G, 3.5G Networks

• Definition of engineering specifications

• Radio and transmission network design

• Site search, identifications, acquisition

• Logistic management

• Core survey, engineering, installation, design, HW DT & traffic DT, using PPTSE & DTH Tools.

• Access (RBS, TN, HC, M-L) survey, engineering, installation, testing, integration & troubleshooting.

• Marconi survey, engineering, installation, testing, integration & troubleshooting.

• Transmission Systems (PDH, SDH optical fiber, DXX).

• VAS System, SUN Server, Intelligent network, prepaid system & billing system.

• Technical start up support & maintenance.

• Operations and Maintenance

• Network optimization.

• Project Management and Planning.

• Shelter assembly, I-Beam installation, AC/DC power installation & alarm systems.

• IBS (In Building Solution).

• Towers/Mast supply and assembly.

• Civil work design and implementation.

• Power system for Access and Core Nodes.

• Quality assurance.
