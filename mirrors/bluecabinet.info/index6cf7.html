Background

"Network monitoring & QoS software, developed to optimizes user experience and provide end-to-end visibility and control from the client device to the network core.

GoS Networks' patented technology, Guarantee of Service, or GoS is a revolutionary approach to Service Quality Assurance. It enables service providers to see traffic as the user experiences it and enforce policies and shape traffic at the real edge of the network, the client device. GoS can monitor, measure and enforce QoE and QoS across any device, delivering a more effective solution than any other technique available today."

Products documentation

GoS 360° enables Network Operators to monitor and control user experience and traffic. Unlike other service assurance solutions, GoS 360° provides visibility and control beyond the network, right down to the end user device. This extends the reach of policy servers and DPI solutions so that traditional network boundaries disappear.

GoS 360° is composed of two elements GoS Agent and GoS Manager. GoS Agent ensures optimal, dynamic QoS configurations to maximise link bandwidth utilisation and application performance. GoS Manager acts as a centralised service level manager collecting statistics and managing hundreds of thousands of GoS Agent devices. It provides detailed statistical reporting and allows dynamic reconfiguration of GoS Agents through an OSS/ BSS/ PCRF interface.

A Solution for the Client Edge A White Paper from GoS Networks

Current solutions leverage Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) a technology that allows Internet Protocol (IP) packets to be inspected as they traverse paths between end points. However, DPI-based solutions are limited as they do not provide full visibility of all traffic from the end points or user devices.

GoS Networks' Management Team

    • John Power - Chief Executive Officer

John has 20 years experience in senior roles in the Telecommunications Industry.

  • John was the founder and Managing Director of J6 Technology prior to joining GoS Networks.

  • Prior to that, John was responsible for setting up high-tech Engineering teams in Ireland for foreign multinationals.

Charles has over 20 years experience in developing communications and signalling systems.

  • Prior to joining GoS Networks he co-founded Jtec Pty Limited in Australia where the legacy signalling and call switching technology that have been acquired by GoS Networks, and now used in the Fusion 1000 and other products, was originally developed.
